Professional Teams

Allen Americans Hockey

The Allen Americans are the Central Hockey League's newest club located in Allen, Texas. The Americans play at the Allen Event Center located in The Village at Allen. With former Stanley Cup champions Mike Modano, Ed Belfour, Craig Ludwig, Richard Matvichuk and Steve Duchesne as owners, the Americans are sure to deliver on and off the ice.

Texas Revolution Football

Playing in the Indoor Football League, the Texas Revolution play their home games inside the Allen Event Center. With the season running opposite of the NFL the Revolution bring REAL TEXAS FOOTBALL to Allen from February through June regularly promoting players on to NFL rosters. The Texas Revolution’s ownership group includes State Senator Ken Paxton and former Heisman Trophy winner and NFL Legend, Tim Brown.

Dallas Sidekicks

The Dallas Sidekicks are a professional indoor soccer team competing in the Major Arena Soccer League. The team plays their home games at the Allen Event Center. The Dallas Sidekicks were founded in 1984 and have won four indoor championships in their tenure. Led by indoor soccer legend and Head Coach and Tatu, the Sidekicks have brought indoor soccer back to Dallas.

Frisco RoughRiders Baseball

The Frisco RoughRiders are the Double-A Affiliate of the Texas Rangers and play their games at beautiful Dr Pepper Ballpark in Frisco, Texas. RoughRiders alumni include MLB All-Stars Ian Kinsler, Elvis Andrus and Matt Harrison. Since 2005, the team has finished first in all of Double-A Baseball in attendance. The RoughRiders are owned by Mandalay Sports Entertainment, winners of the “Baseball Organization of the Year” in 2008 by Ballpark Digest.

Texas Legends Basketball

The Texas Legends are an American professional basketball team based in Frisco, Texas. They play in the Central Division in the NBA Development League (NBA D-League), a minor league basketball organization owned and run by the National Basketball Association (NBA). The Legends play their home games at the Dr Pepper Arena. The Legends are affiliated with the Dallas Mavericks of the NBA.
