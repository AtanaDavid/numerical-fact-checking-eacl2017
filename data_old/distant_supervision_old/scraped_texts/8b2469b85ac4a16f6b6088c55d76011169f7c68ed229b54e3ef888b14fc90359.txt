Winner 15 is a sand filled tufted tennis surface, classified to ITF Category 4 Medium Fast. Manufactured with fibrillated polypropylene pile yarn to a height of 15mm, whether beginner or professional level this surface offers a positive grip, comfort, and a degree of spin. A low maintenance surface with the option for permanently inlaid lines, Winner 15 is an excellent choice for a club wanting a dedicated surface for tennis.
• High performance sand dressed surface• Positive grip underfoot• Classified to ITF Category 4 Medium Fast• Available in green, terracotta
