Jensen Buchanan was born on July 18, 1962 in Montgomery, Alabama, USA. She is an actress, known for Another World (1964), As the World Turns (1956) and One Life to Live (1968). She has been married to Sam Gores since 2003. She was previously married to Gray O'Brien. Wikipedia
