Miley Cyrus vs. Rihanna – Hottest Star of 2014 [FINALS]

by Maggie Malach July 7, 2014 3:12 PM
SHARE
TWEET
EMAIL
REDDIT
EMAIL
REDDIT

After weeks of voting, it’s all come down to this: Miley Cyrus or Rihanna for Hottest Female Star of 2014! In previous rounds, Miley ousted Iggy Azalea and Demi Lovato before moving to the final round. Rihanna, for her part, bested Taylor Swift and Ariana Grande. It’s up to the fans to decide: Will Miley or Rihanna be crowned the Hottest Star of 2014?

Miley has proven time and time again that she definitely can’t be stopped. Whether it’s releasing iconic songs or raising eyebrows with her provocative Bangerz Tour, the singer is always pushing boundaries! Of course, one of her most standout qualities is her devotion to her fans. Even as Miley continues to get hotter and hotter, she never forgets her loyal Smilers. She’s the ultimate performer, and undoubtedly deserving of the title of Hottest Star!

On the other hand, Rihanna is practically synonymous with the word “hot.” From her sultry music videos to her even sultrier Instagram pics, she oozes sex appeal. One of Rihanna’s most buzzed-about moments of 2014 was when she walked the CFDAs red carpet in a shimmering sheer dress. Naturally, she looked amazing. Rihanna knows one of the biggest keys to being hot is confidence — and she never lets us down!

Who should be crowned the Hottest Female Star of 2014? You can vote for Miley or Rihanna once an hour until the polls close at 12PM ET on July 20.
