FCA TEAMS MAKING STRONG STATEMENT THIS INDOOR SEASON

All FCA teams are playing strong this Indoor Season in 2 of the best indoors competiton in the Chicago. Out... of the FCA 10 Teams playing this season 8 Teams are playing in Semi's.
Our other 2 tams that didnt make Semi's were unlucky in playing favorites in their 1/4 finals. U10 & U12 lost very close games and deserved to win. What is more impressive is that 1/2 the roster of those 2 teams are 1 year younger.
Our U12's lost by 1 goal to Chicago Mustangs in 1/4 final.
Our U16's beat the Chicago Mustangs as well.
These U12 & U16 Chicago Mustangs teams were coached by this same Club's Indoor Pro Team Coach, Armando Gamboa, who took was PASL's Coach of the Year & League Champs.
We realize that many other clubs play us with overage players but we will never do the same. We play to improve our players. This is why we play so many up an age. Getting these results are just a bonus. The most exiting part of our success is seeing so many people outside the club exited of watching our talent play. They come to see our great individual and team play. We take pride in our players that can take over a game at any moment and realize that this is exactly what our fans want to see!!
Come and watch us play at Soccer City & Grand Sports Arena !!!
