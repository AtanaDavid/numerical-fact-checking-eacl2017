Jennifer Lawrence To Produce First Movie

  • Share to Facebook
  • Tweet to Twitter
  • Share to Google plus
Thursday, November 14, 2013 - 09:40

She's the hottest female star in Hollywood, but Oscar winning actress Jennifer Lawrence is now preparing to step behind the camera to produce her first movie.

The 23-year-old star, who was in London on Monday [uuid=30b5a676-7a44-4e74-a913-d45fc42578bb]The Hunger Games: Catching Fire, is to work as a producer on a big-screen adaptation of novel The Glass Castle.

She explained to the Associated Press: "I don't know if I will be any good, but I'm trying it.

"So far I am not sure if I am because I am very instinctual, but not very verbal. But producing is something that I have really wanted to do."

And she explained that her mum had talked her into giving it a go.

"My mother is like the lucky charm with these kinds of things. She read Winter's Bone and Hunger Games and when I read The Glass Castle I thought it was amazing, so we found Gil Netter, the producer who had the rights to (the book).

"He and I started talking and now we are developing. We have a director and a writer and it's getting going."
