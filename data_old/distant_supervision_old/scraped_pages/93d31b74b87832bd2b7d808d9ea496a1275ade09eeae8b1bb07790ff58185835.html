Stoke 0-4 Spurs - MOTM result

Posted on 19 April 2016  - 10:30

Our supporters' Man of the Match for the resounding win at Stoke goes to...the whole team!

 
 

Every player could have been on the list so we went for double goalscorers Harry Kane and Dele Alli, Erik Lamela and then provided the 'whole team' option.

And with over 57% of over 30,000 votes cast on Facebook, Twitter and the Match Centre, fans agreed that the whole team had to take the MOTM honours.

#TogetherTHFC pic.twitter.com/SNXaUlVoYQ

— Tottenham Hotspur (@SpursOfficial) April 18, 2016

MAN OF THE MATCH - STOKE AWAY

The whole team - 57.93%

Dele Alli - 22.21%

Harry Kane - 12.80%

Erik Lamela - 7.06%

Thanks for all your votes!
