1
Lamar Jackson
QB | Louisville
Next Game: 11/12 vs. WAKE
Another game, another stat line out of a video game for Jackson. He had seven total touchdowns in Louisville's road win over Boston College. He has now accounted for at least four touchdowns in seven of the Cardinals' nine games. He has 45 total touchdowns this season, on pace to break the FBS record Colt Brennan set in Hawaii in 2006 (63 in 14 games). He missed two first-half drives with an arm cramp but said he was "100 percent" after returning.

Peppers wasn't spectacular in any facet of Saturday's big win over Maryland, but he continues to do a little bit of everything.
