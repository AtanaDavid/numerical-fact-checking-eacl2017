'Pray for Nice': Social media users react to Bastille Day attack

Cartoonists and artists have posted emotive images in response to the attack in Nice which killed 84 people, many of them children.

Some 50 people remain critically injured after a lorry driver ploughed into crowds celebrating France's national Bastille Day in Nice on Thursday evening.

Under the trending hashtag 'Pray for Nice', illustrator Carlos Latuff posted a provocative image of doll crying next to the body of a child.

The drawing was shared by broadcaster Jeremy Vine next to the photograph that had inspired it.
