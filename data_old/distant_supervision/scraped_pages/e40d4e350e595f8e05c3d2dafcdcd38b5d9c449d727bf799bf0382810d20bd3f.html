ABC Gravel: (aggregate binder course)

ABC gravel is a rock mulch used primarily for a road base compaction material or used as a subbase under concrete or asphalt. ABC gravel is also very effective in improving muddy driveways roads or parking lots. It has a variety of sieve sizes and fines which help "lock it in" for the stable base described above. It can be compacted under paver stones well.



3/4" Aggregate:

Rock used in septic systems and leach fields for drainage purposes. We just started carrying this 3/4" Aggregate again.


Mortar Sand / Masonry Sand / Sand:

Mortar Sand is a fine grade washed sand used in mortar and stucco applications. Masonry Sand is also an excellent general purpose sand, or for use in leveling brick pavers, sod or turf lawns. It can also be mixed with some of our mulch product used in gardening to help supply drainage.



Sandy Loam:

Sandy Loam is 33% screened fill dirt, 33% mulch, 33% manure. Sandy Loam is a great medium for vegetable gardens and other applications that utilize the decomposition of the products to supply nutrients to soils.



Arena Sand:

Arena Sand is used in horse arenas. When riding in an arena, footing is one of the most important aspects of your horse's performance, don't settle for a second-rate arena sand that could compromise you and your horse's safety and comfort.
