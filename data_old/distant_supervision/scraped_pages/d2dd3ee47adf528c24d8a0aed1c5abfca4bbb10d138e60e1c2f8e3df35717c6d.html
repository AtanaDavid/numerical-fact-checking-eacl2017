Walking

Bled is a good base from which to partake in any number of hikes into the eastern tranche of the Julian Alps. Although there is some good walking around Bled for all levels of expertise, there are some very enjoyable walks in the immediate vicinity of the lake. Velika Osojnica; the south western corner of the lake or a gentler, alternative walk up to Straza, the south west of the Grand Hotel Toplice. Take a stroll around the lake, climb up to the castle for some spectacular views over the lake. The hills surrounding Bled offer a choice of interesting walks. Also for guests staying at the Hotel Ribno many more walks along the Sava Bohinjka Valley can be taken directly from the hotel's doorstep.

My favourite destination

My favorite destination has to be a place with mountains close by. So Slovenia and Lake Bled is the place to be for me. Fresh air, crystal clear waters, white mountain peaks,unspoiled nature, meadows full of alpine flowers ...a hidden jewel in central Europe which is still waiting to be discovered.Â 

Why is it my favourite?

I have traveled around, and i have seen many different places, but there is no place like home. I am very proud of our beautiful country which has so much to offer...from mountain peaks of Julian Alps, unspoiled nature, lots of national parks, ..and a bit of a Mediterranean feeling on our coast line.

What I do my spare time

Being a Slovenian, in winter, spending our spear times is being on skies. That is a rule! In summers, again Slovenia has so much to offer regarding the outdoor adventures, and everything is at your door step. So in the summer I do a lot of hiking, mountain biking and rock climbingÂ 

A fun fact

Finally Slovenia has found it's place in the world map. Summers are getting more and more popular. There are a lots of alpine clear waters, which are known for white water rafting, canyoning...so this will be my plan to try some of the similar adventures. First go will be Bled adventure park (just to get into the "adventure spirit").
