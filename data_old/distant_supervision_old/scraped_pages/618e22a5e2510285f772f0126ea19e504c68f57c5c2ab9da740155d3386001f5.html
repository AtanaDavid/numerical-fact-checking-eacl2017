WATCH THE LIONS LIVE SESSIONS

Kicking off Lions Live, rock star creative agency Snask will explain why it’s not enough to have beliefs: you have to stand up for them too. Join them as they reveal through a crazy cacophony of music and musings how to “make enemies and gain fans”. 

ON NOW: SNASK

Tune in now to see rock star creative agency Snask explain why it’s not enough to have beliefs: you have to stand up for them too.

UP NEXT ON LIONS LIVE: MEMBRAIN

Up next on Lions Live, the man dubbed the “greatest magician alive”, David Copperfield, will be in conversation with memBrain’s Kevin Hart, sharing the art of compelling storytelling and performing a world-exclusive illusion.

ON NOW: MEMBRAIN

Magician David Copperfield joins memBrain’s Kevin Hart to share the art of storytelling and perform a world-exclusive illusion.

UP NEXT ON LIONS LIVE: HAKUHODO

Tune in now to see Hakuhodo Inc’s worldwide CCO Masaru Kitakaze analyse the makeup of successful modern agency models.

ON NOW: HAKUHODO

Tune in now to see Hakuhodo Inc’s worldwide CCO Masaru Kitakaze analyse successful modern agency models, presenting case studies of groundbreaking projects and decode the creative DNA of the small teams that produced them.

UP NEXT ON LIONS LIVE: FLIPBOARD

Up next on Lions Live, The New Yorker’s Ken Auletta will be joined by Flipboard CEO Mike McCue, considering a world in which machines define what is culturally relevant, the rise of algorithms and the fate of serendipity.

ON NOW: FLIPBOARD

Tune in now to see The New Yorker’s Ken Auletta and Flipboard CEO Mike McCue consider algorithms and the art of curating today.

UP NEXT ON LIONS LIVE: DENTSU AND RAZORFISH & CONTAGIOUS

Up next on Lions Live, Dentsu will take you on a global tour of design, followed by Razorfish and Contagious’s mission to find out whether there’s a formula for creativity, once and for all.

ON NOW: DENTSU

Tune in now to join Dentsu’s 28-time Lion winner Yoshihiro Yagi on a global tour of design, revealing what we can learn from designers and the way they think around the world.

UP NEXT ON LIONS LIVE: RAZORFISH & CONTAGIOUS

Up next on Lions Live, Razorfish and Contagious will attempt to find out whether there’s a formula for creativity, once and for all. Are particular types of category, client or agency more likely to cultivate creativity? Data science should help us find out.
