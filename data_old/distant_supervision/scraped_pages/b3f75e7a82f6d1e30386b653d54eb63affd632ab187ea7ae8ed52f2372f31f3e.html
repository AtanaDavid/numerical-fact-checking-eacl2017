Wikipedia: Hors catégorie (HC) is a French term used in bicycle races to designate a climb that is "beyond categorization". The term was originally used for those mountain roads where cars were not expected to be able to pass. The HC climb is the most difficult type of climb. It is more demanding than a Category 1 climb which in turn is more demanding than a Category 2 climb and so on. The easiest category is Category 4.

The entire BRADSHAW GRINDER course could be rated Hors catégorie! Just sixteen days away! This will be an exciting event in absolutely spectacular surroundings.
