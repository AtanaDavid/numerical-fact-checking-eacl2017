Home

Welcome to the British Carp Angling Championships (BCAC) website and one of the most high profile competitions in the worldwide angling calendar. As we enter our 19th year we are delighted to continue to improve the experience for all involved.

The letter next to each of the qualifying rounds relates to which of the semi-finals that you would fish should you qualify so please consider this in choosing your qualifier venue.

Not only will you have the chance of being crowned British Champions but also getting your hands on cash prizes;

1st Place £20,000

2nd Place £3,000

3rd Place £2,000

 

Carp Couture Clothing

1st, 2nd and 3rd place will receive prizes from Carp Couture Clothing

 

Reuben Heaton

The winners will be given personalised Reuben Heaton Silver Scale which are exclusive to the BCAC.

 

Carp Team England 

Winners will be awarded a place in the 2018 Carp Team England Squad.
