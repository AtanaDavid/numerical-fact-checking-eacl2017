AZ Real Estate

AZ Real Estate Limited was founded and registered in the UK in 2012 by Ms. Alisa Zotimova. Based in London and Moscow, the company works with real estate projects on independent basis using Ms. Zotimova’s network across the EMEA region.

It provides bespoke London investment buy-side advice with an individual approach for inward investment across all property sectors including office, mixed-use and residential. The projects and sites are often sourced off-market to ensure the best opportunities and returns. This is done with discretion, in English or Russian and with access to the full scope of deals available.
