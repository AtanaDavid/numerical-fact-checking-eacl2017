The Surfaces in Biomaterials Foundation annually selects an individual who has made significant contributions to the biomaterials field. It is the highest award given by the Foundation. The first award was presented in 1991 to Buddy Ratner, University of Washington. The award is presented at the Biointerface Conference.
(No award was presented in 1992, 1993, 1994, 1995, 1997 or 1998)
