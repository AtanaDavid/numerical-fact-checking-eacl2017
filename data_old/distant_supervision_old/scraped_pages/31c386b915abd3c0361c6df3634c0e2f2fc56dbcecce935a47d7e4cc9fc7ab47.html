All future fixtures from here onwards have been removed from the site on the orders of Football DataCo Ltd who told us that we would have to pay over £1,200 to continue showing fixtures on our site. Naturally, we will not pay this figure which we consider to be extortionate, simply for us to do a favour to the football clubs local to our area.
