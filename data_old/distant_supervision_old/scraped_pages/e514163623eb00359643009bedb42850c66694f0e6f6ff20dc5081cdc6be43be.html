My Notes

Many upgrades to this wonderful townhome. Kitchen has DuPont Zodiaq easy clean countertops and newer appliances with an over the range microwave, expanded cabinetry/pantry, and an eat-in kitchen. The townhome has a very open floor plan with vaulted ceiling and gas fireplace in the living room. The loft / study overlooks the living room. The master suite is on the first floor with a spacious bath and walk-in closet. There is a first floor half-bath / utility room with newer washer/dryer. All bathrooms are tiled. Designer window treatments throughout. Hypoallergenic carpeting in all bedrooms, Pergo flooring in common areas. Security system ready. High efficiency central air with zone capability. Spacious attic with lots storage space. Attic is floored and well lit and has a pull down stairway. This townhome is in a great location being very close to Landstown Commons and Farmers Market shopping, Virginia Beach Amphitheater / Soccer Sportsplex, TCC, Sentara at Princess Anne medical center, Oceana NAS, and CDSA Dam Neck. Excellent school district with Christopher Farms Elementary, Landstown Middle and High schools all just a few minutes away. Small storage shed in back yard. Front yard lawn maintenance is included. Pets negotiable. Located in a safe family-oriented park-like setting with access to Parkside Green community recreation and playground areas.Credit check required. Smoking indoors is not allowed.
