Nottingham Forest Ladies FC: 
Regional Talent Club 
Part-Time Paid and Voluntary Positions Available:
 
Roles Available for Application: 
Goalkeeping Coach
Coaching Positions
 
Following a six-month application process Nottingham Forest Ladies FC were recently awarded one of only 34 clubs awarded with a prestigious FA Regional Talent Club (RTC) Licenses which will be responsible for the delivery of elite girls’ football development. 
 
RTCs have replaced the former Centre of Excellence model and will deliver enhanced coaching and support designed to improve the development of elite female players through the FA Girls’ England talent pathway with age groups of U12, U14 and U16.
As a result, Nottingham Forest Ladies FC have some exciting roles available. 
 
Goalkeeping Coach: 
Responsible for the goalkeeping coaching for the RTC ensuring the co-ordination, organization and delivery of well-structured and professional coaching sessions on training nights and match days.
 
Applicants must meet the following minimum requirements: 
·  Hold a minimum FA Level 2 Goalkeeping Award
·  Hold both FA Youth Award Modules 1 and 2
·  BFAS or 1st Aid equivalent
·  Hold an up to date FA Safeguarding certification
·  Be a member of the FA Coaching License

Nottingham Forest Ladies FC 
Applications Invited for: 
Senior Team Physio x 1 
Senior Team Sports Therapist x 1 
Junior Team Physio x1 
 
Nottingham Forest Ladies FC are seeking to recruit 2 x Physiotherapists and 1 x Sports Therapists to provide medical care for the senior squad and a weekly clinic for the junior teams. We are looking for recent physiotherapy graduates who would like an opportunity to work in a sports setting or current 3rd year physiotherapist/ sports rehabilitation undergraduate students who can work under the supervision of the clubs current staff. 
 
Nottingham Forest Ladies is a well-established club with a 25 year+ history, whose senior first team currently play in the FA Women's Premier League Northern Division. Games are played at Basford United FC and the seniors train twice weekly (Tuesday / Thursday). 
 
These roles are advertised as unpaid but afford a great opportunity to work with one of the most successful and well established flagship women's football clubs in the East Midlands. The roles offer a great opportunity and experience for those currently in study. 
 
For more information of to discuss the opportunity applicable, please contact Operations Manager Steven Gray in the first instance. 
To apply please forward an up to date CV with covering letter to Operations Manager Steven Gray.
