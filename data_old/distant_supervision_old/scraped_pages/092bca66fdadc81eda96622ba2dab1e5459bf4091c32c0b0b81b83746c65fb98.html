"Check this section for all the Leeds United results and scorers, the final placings in the league table, all the attendances and more Leeds Statistics. United's first ever professional game was played in 1920 on August 28th against Port Vale, our statistics section goes from this date up to the last game of last season. Also included is Leeds City's results, final placings, attendances and more in the same format. Leeds City with Gilbert Gillies as manager have the honour of playing the first ever 'league' football game in Leeds. Playing Bradford City on September 2nd 1905. City's section takes us from this date up until their controversial end in 1919. Interestingly both Leeds City and Leeds United lost their first ever game in the first season but both teams won the last game of that season."
