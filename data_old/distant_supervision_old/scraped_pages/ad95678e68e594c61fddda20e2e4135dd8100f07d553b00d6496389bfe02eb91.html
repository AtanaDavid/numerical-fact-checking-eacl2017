Are you the next Website of the Year winner?

For a period of 6 weeks, from 3 October until 11 November, the British public will rate the content, navigation, design and recommendation intention of the nominated websites.

Winning a Website of the Year award is the ultimate proof that your work is appreciated by the public. It adds value to the success you have already achieved and will inspire greater loyalty and trust in your visitors and stakeholders. You will be entitled to use the logo in your communication. Participation is free of charge.

How to participate:

To get more votes for your website, we advise you to promote your nomination. To help you do so, we have compiled a media kit which contains standard banners and our logo. You can download the media kit by clicking on the link below. The banners are available in 6 different IAB sizes. Please make sure that the banner always links to your own personal voting page. In case the banners do not match your corporate identity or taste, feel free to modify the source file.

About Website of the Year:

The Website of the Year award is the online people’s choice award for the best and most popular websites. The election is an international award and is organized in 7 different countries: France, Germany, India, Italy, the Netherlands, Spain and the United Kingdom. In 2015, voters casted over 3 million votes in 7 different elections. The Website of the Year award is independent and objective and is organized by MetrixLab. Website: websiteoftheyearawards.com.
