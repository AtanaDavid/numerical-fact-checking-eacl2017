30 June 2014 ·

Wanted to give everyone a quick update on the going on for the Chicago game.

Thursday night we will have be having a "mixer" for all supporters to meet up and have some drinks/laughs. We are going to be releasing full details shortly.

Friday there will be a training session for the team down at Toyota Park it will be at 4 p.m. Later that night at 8 p.m. we will be having a Q&A at the Chicago Spurs home bar Atlantic Bar!

...

The Q&A will feature Ledley King, and a coach whose name rhymes with Leffen Reund. Unfortunately space will be first come first serve. We will try to accommodate as many as we can but the bar has limitations.

On Saturday it gets real. The Chicago Fire will be taking on Tottenham. The Fire runs buses from different bars in the city and tickets can be purchased from - Pub to Pitch

The buses with the most spurs supporters will be leaving from the Atlantic Bar. But we will have Spurs bus captains on each bus helping guide all of the yiddos to the Away End parking Lot shindig.

Saturday prior to the game at 5p.m. we will be taking on Section 8 (the Chicago Fire's supporters group) in a friendly game of footy. We will be playing on the Fire's training field which is located in the parking lot at Toyota Park. We will be wearing white, if you have a kit please wear it. If not please bring a white colored shirt. Also it is a turf pitch so please bring turf/indoor shoes. Cleats are not allowed on the pitch.

After the game there will be an after party at the Atlantic Bar.

If you have any questions please feel free to ask them here or on Twitter @chicagospurs or theboard@chicagospurs.org

Also if you have any questions about what to do in Chicago while you are here we have some suggestions for you!

P.S. - there maybe an error when trying to get a bus ticket. We are aware and we have alerted the fire to the issue. Will advise when more information is available.

P.S.S - Tomorrow July 1st at 7pm, we will be having an Open Board meeting at the Atlantic. If you are in the Chicago area you should come by.

HERE IS WHAT'S UP WITH SPURS-FIRE TICKETS
I talked to the people at the Chicago Fire and they said tickets in the Spurs supporters section are still available (about 175 remaining, when I spoke with her at 11 a.m.), but the problem is just so many people trying to buy at once.

Here's what to do: keep clicking the "find tickets" button at www.chicago-fire.com/SpursSupporters until you get through (sometimes it says group tickets are no longer available--just ignore that), and ...you should be able to grab tickets at least for a little while longer. Protip: Make sure you're using an up-to-date browser like Chrome or Firefox or Safari. If the section fills out, the Fire is going to open up some more blocks, but it takes a little time to do that because they have to talk with their security managers about staffing it, etc. So more Spurs supporters seats should open up throughout the day. If you still really can't get tickets anywhere by this afternoon, e-mail us at theboard@chicagospurs.org and we'll see if we can help further.

We are thrilled to host Tottenham Hotspur when they play the Chicago Fire on Saturday, 26 July 2014 at Toyota Park. The match is part of Spurs’ summer tour, which will also include a friendly versus the Seattle Sounders a week earlier on July 19. This is the first time Spurs have come to the Windy City and we are looking forward to making it a great weekend for the Tottenham Hotspur players and staff, along with all the Spurs supporters who come to the match.

http://www.totte...nhamhotspur.com/…/spurs-heading-back-to-us…/

The Chicago Fire have told us that there will be a designated area for Spurs supporters at the match, and we are working with the ticket office to find out how purchasing tickets in that section will work. We will let everyone know the details as soon as they are available.

Spurs have also indicated that there will be events like meet-and-greets, open training sessions, and supporter events with Spurs legends, but they have not indicated where or when those events will be. Stay tuned for updates!
