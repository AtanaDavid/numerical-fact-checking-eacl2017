run·ner-up

(rŭn′ər-ŭp′)
n. pl. run·ners-up (rŭn′ərz-)
One that takes second place or finishes just behind another, as in a game, sport, or other competition.

runner-up

n, pl runners-up
(General Sporting Terms) a contestant finishing a race or competition in second place

run′ner-up′



n., pl. run•ners-up.
1. the competitor, player, or team finishing in second place.
2. runners-up, the competitors who place second, third, and fourth, or in the top ten.
[1835–45]
