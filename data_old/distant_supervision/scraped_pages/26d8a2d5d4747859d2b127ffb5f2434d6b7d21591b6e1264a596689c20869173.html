The Original European React Conference

ReactEurope is back on 18-19 May 2017 to bring you the best and most passionate people from the very core team to the coolest people from the community we love. The conference aims to give talks that inspire and explore new futuristic ideas dealing with all the techs we enjoy from the React ecosystem such as React.js, React Native, GraphQL, Relay, Universal apps, Webpack, inline CSS and more.

ReactEurope is also a great occasion to socialize, meet new people and old friends, hack together, taste delicious food and have fun in the beautiful city of Paris.

Join us at ReactEurope Conf to shape the future of client-side, mobile and universal applications!

Do you do refunds?

Ticket Refund

We do refund tickets with a 5% fee until October 15. However, it is possible to transfer your ticket at all time.

Ticket Transfer

It is possible to transfer your ticket to a peer. Just open the email you received when you bought your ticket and follow the link to edit your ticket details.

What do I get for each conference ticket?

All tickets give access to the two-day conference, this includes typical French breakfasts, tasty lunches and refreshments through the day (both alcoholic and not). We also have swag such as t-shirts, prizes and more, not to forget - the After Party on May 18th. Entrance to social events around the conference to be announced later. Workshops are sold separately and do not include access to the conference.

Workshops details

Want to get some hands-on experience with the latest in React.js and React Native, delivered by true experts?

1) React Workshop with Max Stoiber and Nik Graf

Learn from the best with a 2 day workshop this May 16th and May 17th from 8:45am to 5:30pm. Max Stoiber and Nik Graf are well known for their work on react boilerplate, carte blanche, draftjs plugins, belle and also for their contributions to the react ecosystem such as react.js, create-react-app, webpack etc. You will learn how to write advanced components, how to set up your tooling and build an app using GraphQL and Relay.

2) React Native Workshop with Brent Vatne and Devin Abbott

Learn from the best with a 2 day workshop this May 16th and May 17th from 8:45am to 5:30pm. Brent Vatne is one of the top contributor to React Native and Devin Abbott is the creator of the Deco IDE. You will learn how to write amazing apps with React Native. You don’t need to have any knowledge in ObjC or Java.

Tickets include breakfast and lunch on both days. They do not include the conference ticket. The workshop requires an intermediary level in React.js (>= 3month experience).
