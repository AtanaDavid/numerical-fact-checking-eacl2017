      Exhibitor Profile

      The exhibitor's profile of Collin County Home And Garden Show will include landscape designers, lawn mowers, home decoration products, kitchenware wares, dining utensils, curtains, mattresses, tiles, bathroom appliances, various species of plants and animals, furniture, tables, lamps and lighting devises.. There will be interior decorators and experts providing valuable advice to the visitors. There will be game rooms, home theater and flooring. The retailers will be present to establish deals with the whole sellers and suppliers.
