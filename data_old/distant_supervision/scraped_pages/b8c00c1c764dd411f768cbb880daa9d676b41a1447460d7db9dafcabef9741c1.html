Fisher Industries Named one of 50 Best Places to Work!

We are pleased to announce that Fisher Industries has been named as one of the 50 Best Places to Work in the northern Plains by Prairie Business Magazine for a 3rd year in a row!  We have made the list every year since the magazine launched the feature in 2014.  Companies were nominated through an anonymous employee survey that outlined work environment, employee benefits, and employee happiness.  Lean more here.
