Puteri Indonesia (literally translates into "Princess of Indonesia"), or also known as Miss Universe Indonesia, is one of the biggest beauty pageant in Indonesia that sends the winner to the Miss Universe pageant.[2][better source needed] The pageant was established in 1992 and is considered the oldest pageant in Indonesia, although the first Puteri Indonesia, Indira Soediro who was crowned as Puteri Indonesia 1992 and participated in Miss ASEAN 1992.[citation needed]

The pageant was traditionally broadcast on Indosiar.[citation needed] In 2007, SCTV Network became the official broadcaster.[3]

History[edit]

Agni Pratistha at the Miss Universe 2007

Puteri Indonesia was starting to choose the winner for Miss Universe in 1995, but by 1996, the winner was forced to withdraw from competing at the Miss Universe pageant by Mrs. Tien Suharto, who was the wife of the late second president of the Republic of Indonesia, Suharto.[citation needed] Starting in 2004 Puteri Indonesia winner was sent to the Miss Universe 2005 in Thailand when was Suharto overthrown.[citation needed]

Winners[edit]

The reign winner of Puteri Indonesia expected to represent Puteri Indonesia foundation motto.[4][better source needed] That is Brain, Beauty and Behavior. As of 2006 the titleholders of Puteri Indonesia divide to the three titles.[5][better source needed] The main Puteri Indonesia automatically crowns as the winner or "Miss Universe Indonesia" and the runners-up award as Puteri Indonesia Lingkungan or "Miss International Indonesia" for Environment Ambassador and Puteri Indonesia Pariwisata or "Miss Supranational Indonesia" for Tourism Ambassador.[6][better source needed]

Awards[edit]

The awards most frequently presented at Puteri Indonesia are Miss Congeniality (1992–present),[7] Miss Talent (1992–present),[8] Miss Intelligence (1995–present),[9] People's Choice Award (1992–2011) and the Favorite Islands' Princesses (2007–present). Other awards that have been presented include Best Traditional Costume (2002, 2015–present), Miss Photogenic (1992-2001), Miss Environment (1993-1996, 2004-2005) Miss Tourism (1994-1996, 2004-2005).

In 1992-1996 Puteri Indonesia Pariwisata (Miss Indonesia Tourism) declared as special award at the Puteri Indonesia contest. Beginning in 2006 together with Puteri Indonesia Lingkungan, the Puteri Indonesia Pariwisata awards to the 2nd Runner-up of Puteri Indonesia and will represent Indonesia at the Miss International pageant. Since 2007-2010 after repositioning title, Puteri Indonesia Pariwisata titleholder did not compete at any International competitions. In 2011-2012 Puteri Indonesia Pariwisata titleholders competed at the Miss Asia Pacific World pageant. In 2013 due to scandals at the pageant, Puteri Indonesia Pariwisata competes at the Miss Supranational pageant.
