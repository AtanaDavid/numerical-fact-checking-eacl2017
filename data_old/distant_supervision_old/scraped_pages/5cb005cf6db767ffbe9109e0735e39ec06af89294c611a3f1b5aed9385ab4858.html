Seventh Annual Collin County Home & Garden Show

Location

Allen Event Center
200 E. Stacy Rd. Allen, TX 75002
Get Directions »
972-678-4646 (phone)
http://alleneventcenter.com/
Neighborhood: Allen
Category: Happenings, Home and Garden: Classes, Shopping

Schedule

  1. February 12, 2016 2pm-7pm
  2. February 13, 2016 10am-7pm
  3. February 14, 2016 11am-5pm

Profile

The seventh Collin County Home & Garden show will return to the Allen Event Center from February 12-14 with a big Bravo name in tow: Jeff Lewis. Design and DIY enthusiasts can browse over 200 booths of custom home builders, remodelers, appliances, cabinets, countertops, spas, pools, interior decorators, and more throughout the weekend. Food trucks and handmade Texas sweets and gifts will also be in attendance, along with the Greyhound Rescue and Collin County Humane Society, who will be assisting pet adoptions all weekend long. Lewis will be in attendance for a Q&A on Saturday from noon to 3pm.
