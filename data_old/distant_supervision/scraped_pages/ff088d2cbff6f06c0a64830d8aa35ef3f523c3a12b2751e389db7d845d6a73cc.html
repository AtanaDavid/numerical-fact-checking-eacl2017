About “One Call Away”

Charlie Puth embraces the ability to call his beloved in “One Call Away”, the lead single his debut album, Nine Track Mind. (Songs from his EP Some Kind Of Love were included on the album as well.)

Structurally, it’s a light ballad, featuring no synth production of any kind. MTV called it an instantly catchy tune.
