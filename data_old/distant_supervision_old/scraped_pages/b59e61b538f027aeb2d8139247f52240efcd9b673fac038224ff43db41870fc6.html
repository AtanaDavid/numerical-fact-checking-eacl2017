Miss England 2016 Final
Event: Miss England 2016 Final Date: 21st July 2016 Location: Southport

Congratulations to the beautiful Miss Hertfordshire 2016 winner Francesca Handley for finishing in the top 5 at the Miss England 2016 Final in Southport.

If this wasn’t an achievement in itself she also picked up the award of Miss England Beauty Pageant Top Model along the way!

Miss Hertfordshire 2016 Final
Event: Miss Hertfordshire 2016 Final Date: 10th April 2016 Location: Stevenage

A successful evening was topped off with the crowning of Francesca Handley as the new face of Miss Hertfordshire 2016.

Danielle Holder picked up 2nd place while Paige Short took the second runner up place respectively.

Well done to all the girls who took part and everyone who helped make it a great evening!
