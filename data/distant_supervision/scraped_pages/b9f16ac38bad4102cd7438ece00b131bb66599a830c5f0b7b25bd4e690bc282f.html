Threadneedle UK Select Trust Limited

The Threadneedle UK Select Trust is part of a comprehensive range of products managed by our highly-regarded UK Equity Investment team. The team's wealth of experience and collaborative approach has been successful in building a strong track record across the investment spectrum.

Why the Threadneedle UK Select Trust Limited

  • Large cap focus with flexibility to adjust to target investment opportunities
  • Wealth of experience: backed by expertise across a diverse range of strategies
  • Well-resourced: UK Equity team of 10 investment professionals

Overview

Key Selling Points

  • The Threadneedle UK Select Trust Limited focuses on large-cap UK holdings but has the flexibility to move up and down the market-cap scale as required to find suitable investment opportunities.
  • Comprehensive UK equity range â€“ Columbia Threadneedle Investments has a comprehensive range of open ended investment funds offering options from large cap holdings to equity income and specialist funds and now manages the Threadneedle UK Select Trust Limited as a closed end vehicle.
  • Well resourced investment team - UK equity team of 10 investment professionals who operate within Columbia Threadneedle Investments' wider team of more than 450 investment professionals.

Key Risks

  • Market Risk: The value of investments can fall as well as rise and investors might not get back the sum originally invested, especially if investments are not held for the long term.
  • Currency Risk: Where investments are made in assets that are denominated in foreign currency, changes in exchange rates may affect the value of the investments.
  • Volatility Risk: The Fund may exhibit significant price volatility.
  • Risk of Gearing: The Threadneedle UK Select Trust Limited (the 'Fund') can borrow money to make further investments. This is commonly referred to as gearing. When this money is repaid, the value of the investment may be insufficient cover for the loan amount, and the Fund will make a loss. The more highly geared the Fund is, the greater the risk of losses within the Fund.

Process

Investment Process

The manager blends both fundamental bottom-up stock research with top-down analysis. A clear understanding of the macroeconomic and thematic background is a vital starting point for building the portfolio.

The Threadneedle UK Select Trust Limited adheres to a clear investment philosophy; alpha is sought from research-driven, bottom up stock selection along with a dynamic approach to drive strong performance across the investment cycle.

Portfolio Manager

Chris Kinder joined the company as a portfolio manager in the UK equities team in 2010. His main responsibilities are the management of the Threadneedle UK Fund and Threadneedle UK Extended Alpha Fund, the co-management of the Threadneedle UK Absolute Alpha Fund and the co-management of a number of institutional portfolios. He is also responsible for coverage of the aerospace and defence, oil services, mining and banking sectors and for idea generation for the wider UK equity team.

Before joining the company Chris was an assistant fund manager at RWC Partners. He has also worked at Govett Investments, gaining experience in smaller companiesâ€™ analysis. Chris is a qualified chartered accountant, having worked at PricewaterhouseCoopers for four years before joining the investment management industry.

Chris holds a degree in Modern European Languages from Durham University. He also holds the Chartered Financial Analyst designation.
