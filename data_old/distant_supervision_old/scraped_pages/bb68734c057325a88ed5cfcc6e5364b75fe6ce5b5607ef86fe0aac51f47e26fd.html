Maxim Name Eva Longoria Woman Of The Year 2014


Tue-07-Jan-14 10:57

Desperate Housewives star Eva Longoria has started 2014 off well by being named Maxim Magazine's Woman of the Year.
 
The 38-year-old actress looks hot on the front of Maxim's January/February issue, the third time she's graced the cover since she was named Hottest Female Star of 2005 and 2006.
 
"I've had a beautiful relationship with Maxim over all these years… When someone sees the cover of this issue, they're going to say, '¡Que rico!' … [You] say it when something is really good."
 
While she enjoys shooting sexy pictures, Eva gets the most pleasure from showing people how different she is to her public persona.
 
"One thing people are usually surprised about when they get to know me is how domestic I am," she explained. "So when you see the sexy Maxim cover and then you put it against me in an apron with flour in my hair, it takes a moment to reconcile that image."
 
Not only an actress, but Eva also received a Master's Degree in Chicano Studies, and is involved in many different interests.
 
"Back in high school I really was the chameleon, too," she revealed. "I got along with the honours math club, then I would go to cheerleading practice, and then I was a band geek as well."
