Match summary

Both through as Germans edge US

The Germans edged Jurgen Klinsmann’s USA 1-0 in Recife’s rain-soaked Arena Pernambuco, a result that takes both sides through and sent Ghana and Portugal crashing out.

Quotes

Recife

Recife is the capital of the state of Pernambuco, in the north-east of Brazil, and the centre of the country's fourth-largest metropolitan area - a conurbation of another 13 cities, including Olinda, with a population of 3.7 million.
