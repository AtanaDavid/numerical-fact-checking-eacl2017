Timothy Fosu-Mensah has said Michael Carrick is a great influence on the younger players at Manchester United.
Carrick, 35, started in United's 3-1 win against Swansea on Sunday but has mostly been a bit-part player under Jose Mourinho this season.
However, Fosu-Mensah, 18, believes the experienced midfielder has an important role to play in helping the younger players at United make the step up to the first team.
