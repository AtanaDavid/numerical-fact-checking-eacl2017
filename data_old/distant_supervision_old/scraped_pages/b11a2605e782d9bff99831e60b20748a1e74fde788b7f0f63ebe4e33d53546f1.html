I Novak "Nole" Djokovic (Serbian:abbreviated as Новак Ђоковић / Novak Đoković) (mibait 22 Mayu 1987) metung yang Serbian a mamyalung tenis a propesyonal ampong sadyang mumuna king yatu. As of 9 2012 (2012 -Expression error: Unrecognized word "anyang".-09)[update], ya kadwa agpang king Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP). Tuturing deng metung kareng mapyang diling talapamyalung king mabilug a panaun; ing kayang tagumpe king hard dela na kaya ing palayong "The King of Hardcourt", at meging sangkan kareng dakal a expertung ituring yang mapyang diling talapamyalung king hard court king kasalesayan.[3][4][5][6][7]

Sinambut yang labing metung a titulung Grand Slam singles, i Djokovic, metung a 13 ATP World Tour Masters 1000 tournament, at kayabe ya king Serbia Davis Cup team a sinambut king 2010.

I Djokovic ing kadwa king ranggu king yatu, kaybat nang Rafael Nadal, a telan na manibat 4 Julio 2011 anggang 8 Julio 2012. Telan ne anggang 9 Julio 2012, anyang elilan neng Roger Federer antimong manimuna king yatu.[8] Ing panga mikaribal da di Roger Federer ampong Rafael Nadal ing tuturing dang mapyang dili king kabilugan ning panaun king pyalung a tennis, at tuturing da pang maki "Trivalry" kareng atlu.[9]
