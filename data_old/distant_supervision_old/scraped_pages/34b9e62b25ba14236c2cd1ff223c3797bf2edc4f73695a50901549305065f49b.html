ISN'T IT

  • By Glamour
  • Wednesday, 4 February 2004

Scarlett Johansson might be the hottest female star in Hollywood right now, but she says she wishes she wasn't. "No one can tell me who the 'It' girl was last year, so it's kind of worrisome," she commented recently. "Who was it? All I know is, once you're the 'It' girl, you are the 'Once-was-an-it-girl girl'." SOURCE: FOX NEWS
