Pennsylvania Farm Show 2016

09 Jan 2016 - 16 Jan 2016
The event is over

Pennsylvania Farm Show Complex & Expo Center
Harrisburg / Pennsylvania / United States of America

Pennsylvania Farm Show is the largest indoor agricultural event held in the United States. This 8 days farm show is being organized by Pennsylvania Department of Agriculture. The venue of this event is Pennsylvania Farm Show Complex & Expo Center which is based in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, United States of America. The event will be held from 09 January 2016, Saturday to 16 January 2016, Saturday. Pennsylvania Farm Show 2016 will showcase a wide range of products and services related to farm & ranch sector from the leading exhibitors. The approximate number of exhibitors that will exhibit at this event is over 300. The frequency of Pennsylvania Farm Show is annual. This farm show has been running since 1917.

Located in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania and opened in 1966, Pennsylvania Farm Show Complex & Expo Center is a large exhibition center and indoor arena for concerts, agricultural exhibitions, farm show, indoor football, exhibits and trade shows. The Complex is 60 acres (240,000 m2), houses 24 acres (97,000 m2) under roof, spread throughout 11 buildings including three arenas.
