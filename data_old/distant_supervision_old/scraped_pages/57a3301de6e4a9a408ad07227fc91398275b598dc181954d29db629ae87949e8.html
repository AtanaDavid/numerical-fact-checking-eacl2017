The Career of Seve Ballesteros

Five times a major champion, 91 worldwide victories, 61 weeks as world no. 1 and the man who rejuvenated European golf after decades of American domination, Seve is the most charismatic golfer Europe has ever produced - a Spanish Arnold Palmer. Sadly both his career, and then his life, were cruelly cut short.
