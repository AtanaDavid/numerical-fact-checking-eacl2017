QPR have announced assistant manager David Oldfield will be overseeing first-team training until the club names Jimmy Floyd Hasselbaink's replacement.
Hasselbaink was sacked as Rangers boss on Saturday following a 1-1 draw at Nottingham Forest, with the London outfit lying 17th in the Championship.
Their next fixture is a home clash with Norwich on November 19.
