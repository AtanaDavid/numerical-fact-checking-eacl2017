 Welcome to Mesa TMS International slag aggregate

The history of using steel slag dates back centuries, but, it has only been since the early 1900’s that TMS International began processing this sustainable steel product. Now, TMS International is the North American leader in slag processing and metal recovery. Our processing plant in Mesa, Arizona provides local companies the opportunity to use an eco friendly, safe, effective aggregate product at a fraction of the cost.

 

Landscapers, building contractors, landfills, and farms are using slag in a variety of ways. Our crushed ABC has been approved by the Maricopa Association of Governments for use in cement and asphalt use.  Click HERE to see how you can benefit from using slag.

 

Slag aggregate is used in a variety of ways.

3/4 Inch

Used as landfill cover, dust control, sub base and more.

1-3 Inch

3 Inch

Used as embankment protection & more. 

Crushed ABC

Meets all MAG spec requirements for crushed ABC.

 Landscape Material

  • Durable aggregate for unpaved parking lots, access roads and road surfaces.

 Erosion Control
