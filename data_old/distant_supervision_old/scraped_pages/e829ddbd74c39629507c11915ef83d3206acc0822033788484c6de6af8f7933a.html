Golfers Who Won After Winning Week Before on Tour

  • Ralph Guldahl, 1939 (won Greater Greenboro Open previous week)
  • Sam Snead, 1949 (Greater Greensboro Open*)
  • Art Wall, 1959 (Azalea Open)
  • Sandy Lyle, 1988 (Greater Greensboro Open)
  • Phil Mickelson, 2006 (BellSouth Classic)

(*GGO was two weeks prior to The Masters in 1949, but was the last Tour event played prior to The Masters.)
