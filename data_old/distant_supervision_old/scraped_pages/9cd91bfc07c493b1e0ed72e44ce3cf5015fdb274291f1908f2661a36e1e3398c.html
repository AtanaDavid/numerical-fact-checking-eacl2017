Organisation

The Best Explained

On its 25th anniversary of honouring world football’s top performers, FIFA has decided to take the game’s most prestigious individual award to a new, modern direction. One that reflects the organisation’s mission of bringing football centre stage.

  • The Best Explained

The Awards Show

The Best FIFA Football Awards™ will be held for the very first time on 9 January 2017, the latest incarnation of the annual awards events that FIFA has been staging since 1991, when the FIFA World Player Gala was founded.

  • The Awards Show

Fan Voting

Do you want to participate in the vote for The Best FIFA Football Awards? Have you already chosen your favourites and want to help them win the prize? In this short and practical guide, we explain how you can make your vote count.
  • Fan Voting

History

Since 1991, FIFA has been organising awards to honour the world’s best footballers. Step back in time to meet the men and women who were judged the greatest in each of the last 25 years.
