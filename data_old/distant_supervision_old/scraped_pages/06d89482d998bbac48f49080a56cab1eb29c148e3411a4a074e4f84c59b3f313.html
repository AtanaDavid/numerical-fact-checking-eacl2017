Tottenham Hotspur

Tottenham Hotspur football club site for all Spurs fans. Provides the biggest database of stats online for club and player as well as the latest fixtures, results and Tottenham Hotspur football news. Spurs were formed in 1882 out of an old cricket club and were non-league when they won their first FA Cup trophy. That was the first of eight FA Cup titles, to which they have added two League Championships (1951 and 1961 - the year they did the Double), four League Cups, two Uefa Cups and the Cup Winners Cup. Tottenham Hotspur football club play at White Hart Lane and are managed by Andre Villas-Boas after the departure of Harry Redknapp. Check back for the latest Tottenham Hotspur football news live scores and exclusive player profiles from the PFA.
