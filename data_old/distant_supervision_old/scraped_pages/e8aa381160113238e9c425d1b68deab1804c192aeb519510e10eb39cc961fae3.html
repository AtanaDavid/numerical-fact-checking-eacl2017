MASL Off the Wall

Members and supporters, use this page to repr...esent your team, share ideas, photos, and history, and keep up with what's going on. Please adhere to the following guidelines when posting/commenting:

* The MASL Off the Wall Group is not affiliated with the Major Arena Soccer League and is independently administered by fans
* Be respectful in your comments
* Keep in mind, posts and comments reflect the views of group members and not necessarily those of the MASL
* Spamming will result in permanent ban
* Vendor advertising other than MASL will result in permanent ban
* Posts about outdoor soccer, international football, and futsal may be removed unless they are in some way relevant to the MASL or indoor soccer
* The moderators and administrators of this group have the right to delete any thread or post
