Andrew Barron "Andy" Murray OBE (born 15 Mey 1987) is a Scottish professional tennis player currently ranked world No. 2 in singles.[3][4] He is a twa-time Grand Slam tournament winner, Olympic champion an Davis Cup champion. Murray is the younger brither o doubles world No. 1 Jamie Murray.

Murray haes reached at least the quarterfinals o all Grand Slam tournaments he haes participated in syne 2011, wi the exception o the 2015 US Open.[5] Murray haes been ranked as British No. 1 syne 27 Februar 2006. He achieved a tap-10 rankin bi the ATP for the first time on 16 Aprile 2007, an reached a career peak o world No. 2 on 17 August 2009.

Murray is the reignin Olympic champion, haein defeatit Roger Federer at the 2012 Olympic Gemmes in straucht sets tae win the gowd medal in the men's singles final, acomin the first British singles champion in ower 100 years. He an aa won a siller medal in the mixed doubles, playin wi Laura Robson.

At the 2012 US Open, Murray became the first British player syne 1977, an the first British man syne 1936, tae win a Grand Slam singles tournament, when he defeatit Novak Djokovic in the final in five sets. This title made him the anly British male tae acome a Grand Slam singles champion durin the Open Era. On 7 Julie 2013, Murray won the 2013 Wimbledon Championships, acomin the first British player tae win a Wimbledon senior singles title syne Virginia Wade in 1977, an the first British man tae win the Men's Singles Championship syne Fred Perry, 77 years previously. Murray is the anly man in history tae hae won Olympic Gowd an the US Open in the same calendar year, as well as the third man tae haud the Gowd Medal an twa majors on different surfaces (efter Andre Agassi an Rafael Nadal). Subsequent tae his success at the Olympics an Wimbledon, Murray wis voted the 2013 BBC Sports Personality of the Year.

Murray haes been the runner-up in aicht ither singles Grand Slam finals: the 2008 US Open, the 2010, 2011, 2013, 2015 an 2016 Australian Opens, the 2012 Wimbledon Championships, an the 2016 French Open losin three tae Roger Federer an five tae Novak Djokovic. He is the first man in the Open Era tae achieve five runner-up finishes at the Australian Open, efter losin tae Djokovic in the final o the 2016 Australian Open. In 2011, Murray became anly the seivent player in the Open Era tae reach the semifinals o all fower Grand Slam tournaments in ane year.[6] Durin the 2015 saison, he became the fift man in tennis history tae hae won ower $40 million in career prize money. Efter reachin the French Open semifinal in 2014, he became the tenth man tae reach twa or more semifinals at each o the fower Majors.[7] Efter reachin the final o the 2016 French Open, Murray became the tenth player in the Open Era tae reach the final o all fower Grand Slam events an joint twelfth on the leet for finals reached.[8] He is anly the seicont British player (efter Fred Perry) o either sex tae hae reached the final o all fower majors.

Murray an aa featurt in Great Britain's Davis Cup winnin team in 2015, winnin 11 matches (8 singles an 3 doubles) as thay secured thair first Davis Cup title syne 1936.[9] Murray wis voted the 2015 BBC Sports Personality of the Year, while the Davis Cup team won the 2015 BBC Sports Personality Team of the Year Award. He haes scored a tot 34 wins an 7 losses wi the British Davis Cup team.

Early and personal life[eedit | eedit soorce]

Murray wis born in Glesga, Scotland, the son o Judith (Erskine) an William Murray.[1] His maternal grandfather, Roy Erskine, wis a professional footballer in the late 1950s.[10] Murray's elder brither, Jamie, is an aa a professional tennis player, playin on the doubles circuit. Murray wis born wi a bipartite patella, whaur the kneecap remains as twa separate bones instead o fusin thegither in early childhood,[11] but wis nae diagnosed until the age o 16. He is seen tae haud his knee due tae the pain caused bi the condeetion an haes pulled oot o events acause o it.[12] Murray began playin tennis at the age o three when his mither Judy teuk him tae play on the local courts.[13] He played in his first competitive tournament at age five an bi the time he wis aicht he wis competin wi adults in the Central Destrict Tennis League.[14]

Murray grew up in Dunblane an attended Dunblane Primary School. He an his brither war present durin the 1996 Dunblane school massacre,[15] when Thomas Hamilton killed 16 childer an a teacher afore shootin himself; Murray teuk cover in a classroom.[16] Murray says he wis too young tae understand what wis happenin an is reluctant tae talk aboot it in interviews, but in his autobiography Hitting Back he states that he attended a youth group run bi Hamilton, an that his mither gae Hamilton lifts in her car.[17] Murray later attended Dunblane High School.[18][19]

Murray's parents split up when he wis anly 10.[20] He believes the impact this haed on him could be the reason behind his competitive spirit.[21] At 15, he wis asked tae train wi Rangers Football Club at thair School o Excellence, but declined, optin tae focus on his tennis career instead.[22] He then decidit tae muive tae Barcelona, Spain. Thare he studied at the Schiller International School an trained on the clay courts o the Sánchez-Casal Academy, coached bi Pato Alvarez.[23] Murray describit this time as "a big sacrifice".[19] His parents haed tae find £40,000 tae pay for his 18-month stay thare.[20] While in Spain, he trained wi Emilio Sánchez, formerly the world No. 1 doubles player.[19]

In Februar 2013, Murray bought Cromlix House for £1.8 million which opened as a 15-room five-starn hotel in Aprile 2014.[24] Later that month Murray wis awairdit Freedom o Stirlin an received an Honorary Doctorate frae the University of Stirling in recognition o his services tae tennis.[25]

Murray began datin Kim Sears, daughter o player-turned-coach Nigel Sears, in 2005.[26][27] Thair engagement wis annoonced in November 2014,[27] an thay mairit on 11 Aprile 2015 at Dunblane Cathedral in his home toun.[28] The couple hae a daughter, Sophia Olivia,[29][30] born on 7 Februar 2016.[31][32]

Junior tennis[eedit | eedit soorce]

Leon Smith, Murray's tennis coach frae 11 tae 17,[23] describit Murray as "unbelievably competitive", while Murray attributes his abilities tae the motivation gained frae losin tae his aulder brither Jamie. In 1999, at the age o 12, Murray won his age group at the Orange Bowl, a prestigious event for junior players.[33]

In Julie 2003, Murray stairtit oot on the Challenger an Futures circuit. In his first tournament, he reached the quarterfinals o the Manchester Challenger.[34] In September, Murray won his first senior title bi takin the Glasgow Futures event.[35] He an aa reached the semi-finals o the Edinburgh Futures event.[36]

For the first sax months o 2004, Murray haed a knee injury an couldn't play.[37]

In Julie 2004, Murray played a Challenger event in Nottingham, whaur he lost tae future Grand Slam finalist Jo-Wilfried Tsonga in the seicont roond.[38] Murray then went on tae win Futures events in Xàtiva[39] an Rome.[40]

In September 2004, he won the Junior US Open an wis selected for the Davis Cup World Group play-off match against Austria later that month;[41] houiver, he wis nae selected tae play. Later that year, he won BBC Young Sports Personality of the Year.[42]

As a junior, Murray reached as high as No. 6 in the world in 2003 (and No. 8 in doubles). In the 2004-instated combined rankings, Murray reached No. 2 in the world.[43]

2005[eedit | eedit soorce]

Murray began 2005 ranked world No. 407,[44] but when he wis in South Americae in Januar, he hurt his back an haed tae tak 3 months off.[37]

In Mairch, he became the youngest Briton tae play in the Davis Cup.[45] Murray turned professional in Aprile an wis gien a wild card entry tae a clay-court tournament in Barcelona, the Open SEAT, whaur he lost in three sets tae Jan Hernych.[46] In Aprile, Murray parted acrimoniously frae his coach Pato Alvarez, complainin aboot his negative attitude.[47] Murray then reached the semi-finals o the boys' French Open whaur he lost in straucht sets tae Marin Čilić.[48]

Mark Petchey agreed tae coach Murray for 4 weeks till the end o Wimbledon, but it metamorphosed intae a full-time poseetion.[47] Gien a wild card tae Queen's,[49] Murray progressed past Santiago Ventura in straucht sets for his first ATP match win.[50] Follaein a seicont roond win against Taylor Dent,[51] he played umwhile Australian Open champion Thomas Johansson in the third roond, losin in three sets efter crampin an twistin his ankle.[52][53] Follaein his performance at Queen's, Murray received a wild card for Wimbledon. Ranked 312, Murray became the first Scot in the Open Era tae reach the third roond o the men's singles tournament at Wimbledon.[54] In the third roond, Murray lost tae 2002 Wimbledon finalist David Nalbandian due tae crampin an fatigue, haein led bi twa sets tae luve.[55]

Follaein Wimbledon, Murray won Challengers events on the haurd courts o Aptos an Binghamton, New York. He then experienced his first Masters event at Cincinnati, whaur he beat Taylor Dent, afore losin in three sets tae world No. 4, Marat Safin. Wi a wild card entry, Murray played Andrei Pavel in the openin roond o the US Open, whaur he recovered frae bein doun twa sets tae ane tae win his first five-set match.[56] Houiver, he lost in the seicont roond tae Arnaud Clément in anither five set contest.[57] Murray wis again selected for the Davis Cup match against Switzerland. He wis picked for the openin singles rubbers, losin in straucht sets tae Stanislas Wawrinka.[58] Murray then made his first ATP final at the Thailand Open whaur he faced world No. 1 Roger Federer. Murray lost in straucht sets.[59]

Murray beat Tim Henman in thair first meetin, at the Basel Swiss Indoors in the first roond, an eventually reached the quarter-finals.[60]

In November, Murray captained Scotland at the inaugural Aberdeen Cup against England led bi Greg Rusedski.[61] This wis an exhibition tournament, an the anly event whaur Murray played Rusedski, thay never met on the Tour. Rusedski beat Murray in the first match, but Murray won the seicont. Scotland defeatit England 4½ - 2½.[62]

He completit the year ranked 64 an wis named the 2005 BBC Scotland Sports Personality of the Year.[63]

2006: British No. 1[eedit | eedit soorce]

The 2006 saison saw Murray compete on the full circuit for the first time an split wi his coach Mark Petchey[64] an team up wi Brad Gilbert.[65]

On 27 Februar, Murray became the British Number 1, endin Tim Henman's seiven-year run. Murray wis nou world No 42, Greg Rusedski No 43, an Tim Henman No 49.[66]

Murray suffered a straucht sets defeat at the Australian Open, tae Argentine Juan Ignacio Chela in the first roond[67] an tae Gaël Monfils at the French Open, in five sets.[68] Murray houiver reached the fowert roond for the first time at baith Wimbledon an the US Open.[69][70]

Murray played in Davis Cup ties against Serbia, Israel an Ukraine. Murray missed the openin singles matches afore losin the doubles as Britain lost thair tie against Serbia.[71] Durin the tie wi Israel, Murray won his rubber an lost the doubles afore pullin oot wi a neck injury afore the reverse singles, as Britain lost the tie.[72][73][74] Against the Ukraine, Murray won baith his singles rubbers, but lost the doubles, as Britain won the tie.[75][76][77]

At the Masters, Murray lost in the first roond in Miami,[78] Monte Carlo an Rome.[79][80] Murray went oot o the tournaments in Indian Wells an Hamburg in the seicont roond.[81][82] Murray reached his first Masters semifinal in Toronto at the Rogers Cup losin tae Richard Gasquet.[83]

At Cincinnati, Murray became anly ane o twa players, alongside Rafael Nadal, tae defeat Roger Federer in 2006, breakin the Swiss starn's 55 match winnin streak on haurd courts.[84] He lost twa roonds later tae Andy Roddick, but broke intae the tap 20 for the first time.[85][86] In the final twa Masters events in Madrid an Paris, Murray exited baith tournaments at the last-16 stage endin his saison, wi losses tae Novak Djokovic an Dominik Hrbatý.[87][88] When the tour reached San Jose, California; Murray defeatit a tap ten player for the first time in the shape o Andy Roddick.[89] Murray went on tae claim the SAP Open title defeatin world No. 11 Lleyton Hewitt.[90] Murray wis an aa a finalist at the Legg Mason Tennis Classic.[91] Playin doubles wi his brither in Bangkok the pair reached the final.[92] Efter the French Open, whaur Murray wis injured again, he revealed that his bones hadn't fully grown, causin him tae suffer frae cramps an back problems.[93]

In November, the Aberdeen Cup wis held for the seicont time, wi Murray leadin the Scotland team an Greg Rusedski captainin England.[94] Scotland won 6½ - 1.[95]

2007: World top 10[eedit | eedit soorce]

Murray reached the fowert roond o the Australian Open, whaur he lost a five-set match against world No. 2, Rafael Nadal.[96]

Follaein the Miami Masters, whaur he reached the semi-finals,[97] Murray became the world No. 10 on 16 Aprile.[98]

The British No 1 sustained tendon damage durin his first roond match at the German Open in Hamburg. Murray wis up 5–1 when he hit a forehaund frae the back o the court an snapped the tendons in his wrist, leavin him oot o action frae 15 Mey until 7 August, tharebi missin Wimbledon.[99] Durin this rest period, Murray rose tae world No. 8, but bi 7 August, he haed dropped tae no 14.[98]

Murray suffered a third roond loss at the US Open. At the Masters tournaments, Murray reached the semifinals o Indian Wells an Miami. At Rome an Cincinnati, Murray exited in the first roond whilst goin oot in the seicont in Canadae. In the final twa masters tournaments, Murray exited in the third roond in Madrid an he went oot in the quarter finals o Paris. Murray won titles in San Jose an St. Petersburg.[100] He an aa reached the final o tournaments in Doha an Metz, finishin the saison ranked 11t in the world.[98]

In November, Murray split wi his coach Brad Gilbert an added a team o experts alang wi Miles Maclagan, his main coach.[101][102]

2008: First Grand Slam final and first Masters titles[eedit | eedit soorce]

In 2008, Murray suffered a first roond loss at the Australian Open tae eventual runner-up Jo-Wilfried Tsonga, an a third roond loss at the French Open tae Nicolás Almagro.[103] Murray then made his first Grand Slam quarter final at Wimbledon afore makin his first final at the US Open. Durin the tournament in New York, Murray claimed his first win ower Nadal. That victory meant that he'd acome the first player frae Britain syne Greg Rusedski in 1997 tae reach a major final.[104] In his first Grand Slam final Murray suffered a straucht sets loss tae Federer.[105][106] At the Beijing Olympics, Murray suffered ane o the worst defeats o his career, losin his first roond singles match tae world No. 77 Yen-hsun Lu o Taiwan in straucht sets. That abject defeat wis still on his mind in a BBC interview five years later – despite an intervenin Olympic gowd medal an a heid-tae-heid win – when he met the same player (nou ranked 75 in the world) in the 2nt roond o Wimbledon 2013.[107]

In the Masters tournaments Murray went oot in roond fower in Indian Wells an the first roond o Miami. In the clay Masters Murray made the third roond o Monte Carlo an Hamburg an the seicont o Rome. On the American haurd court swin Murray made the semi finals o Toronto afore winnin his first Masters shield in Cincinnati. He added anither shield tae his collection in Madrid;[108] afore losin in the quarter finals o Paris. Nou at No. 4 in the world, Murray qualified for the first time for the Masters Cup. He played well in defeatin an injured Federer[109] but lost tae Davydenko in the semi-finals.[110] Murray ended 2008 ranked fowert in the world. Murray an aa won tournaments in Doha, Marseille an St Petersburg.

2009: World No. 2[eedit | eedit soorce]

Murray made the quarter-finals of the 2009 French Open

Murray opened the 2009 saison wi a successfu defence o his title at the Qatar Open in Doha, defeatin Andy Roddick in straucht sets.[111] At the Australian Open, Murray made it tae the fowert roond, losin tae Fernando Verdasco.[112] Murray won his eleventh career title in Rotterdam, defeatin No. 1, Nadal in the three sets.[113] Murray next went tae Dubai but withdrew afore the quarterfinals wi a re-occurrence o a virus that haed affected him at the Australian Open.[114] The virus caused Murray tae miss a Davis Cup tie in Glesga. Murray then lost in the finals tae Nadal at Indian Wells,[115] but won a week later in Miami ower Novak Djokovic for anither masters title.

In the lead-up tae the French Open, Murray beat world No. 9, Nikolay Davydenko at the Monte Carlo Masters, the first time he haed beaten a tap ten player on clay,[116] tho he lost tae Nadal in the semis. Murray wis upset in roond twa o the Rome Masters bi qualifier Juan Mónaco, an he reached the quarterfinals o the Madrid Masters, losin tae Juan Martín del Potro. Durin this time Murray achieved the heichest ever rankin o a British male in the Open Era when he became world No. 3 on 11 Mey 2009.[117] Murray reached the quarterfinals o the French Open, but wis defeatit bi Fernando González in fower sets.

Murray won for the first time on gress at Queen's an became the first British winner o the tournament syne 1938. In the final Murray defeatit American James Blake.[118] At Wimbledon, against Stanislas Wawrinka, Murray's fowert roond match wis the first match tae be played entirely under Wimbledon's retractable roof, an aa enablin it tae be the latest finishin match ever at Wimbledon.[119] Houiver Murray lost a tight semifinal tae Andy Roddick.

Murray returned tae action in Montreal, defeatin del Potro in three sets tae tak the title.[120] Efter this victory, he owerteuk Nadal in the rankings an held the number twa poseetion until the stairt o the US Open.[121] Murray followed the Masters win playin at the Cincinnati Masters, whaur he lost tae Federer. At the US Open, Murray wis hampered bi a wrist injury an suffered a straucht-sets loss tae Čilić.[122] Murray won baith his singles matches, an lost at doubles in the Davis Cup against Poland,[123] but wis then forced tae miss sax weeks wi a wrist injury.[124]

In November, Murray won at Valencia,[124][125] but bowed oot in roond twa o the Paris Masters. Tae end the saison, Murray did nae mak it oot o the roond robin at the World Tour Finals in London.[126] Template:Quotebox

2010: Australian Open runner-up and two Masters titles[eedit | eedit soorce]

Murray an Laura Robson represented Britain at the Hopman Cup. The pair progressed tae the final, whaur thay war beaten bi Spain.[127] At the Australian Open Murray beat Nadal an Čilić afore losin in the final tae world No. 1 Roger Federer.[128] At the BNP Paribas Open in Indian Wells, Murray reached the quarterfinals, losin tae Robin Söderling in straucht sets. Murray next played at the 2010 Sony Ericsson Open, but lost his first match o the tournament tae Mardy Fish, afterwards sayin that his mind hadn't been fully on tennis.[129] At the Monte-Carlo Rolex Masters, Murray suffered anither first match loss, this time tae Philipp Kohlschreiber. He an aa entered the doubles competeetion wi Ross Hutchins, but thay lost tae the Bryan Brothers on a champions tie-breaker. Murray reached the third roond in the Rome Masters, an the quarterfinals at the Madrid Masters, losin baith times tae David Ferrer.

Murray reached his seicont Grand Slam Final in Australie.

Efter playin an exhibition match, Murray stairtit the French Open wi three tough wins, afore losin in straucht sets tae Tomáš Berdych in the fowert roond.[130] In London, Murray progressed tae the third roond, whaur he faced Mardy Fish. At 3–3 in the final set wi momentum goin Murray's way (he haed juist come back frae 3–0 doun), the match wis cried off for bad licht, leavin Murray fuming. Comin back the next day, Murray wis edged oot bi the eventual finalist in a tie-breaker for his seicont defeat bi him in the year.[131][132] At Wimbledon, Murray progressed tae the semi-finals, losin tae Rafael Nadal in straucht sets.[133] On 27 Julie 2010, Andy Murray an his coach Maclagan split, an Murray replaced him wi Àlex Corretja.[134]

Stairtin the US haurd-court saison wi the 2010 Farmers Classic, Murray reached the final but lost against Sam Querrey in three sets. This wis his first loss tae Querrey in five career meetins.[135] In Canadae, Murray became the first player syne Andre Agassi in 1995 tae defend the Canadian Masters. Murray defeatit Nadal an then Federer in straucht sets, endin his aicht-month title drocht.[136] At the Cincinnati Masters, Murray first complained aboot the speed o the court,[137] an then in a quarterfinal match wi Fish, Murray complained that the organisers refused tae put the match on later in the day[138] Wi temperatures reachin 33 °C in the shade, Murray won the first set in a tie-breaker but began tae feel ill. The doctor wis cried on court tae actively cuil Murray doun. Murray admitted efter the match that he haed considered retiring. He lost the seicont set, but forced a final-set tie-breaker, afore Fish won.[139] Efter losin tae Stanislas Wawrinka in the third roond o the US Open, questions aboot Murray's conditionin arose, as he cried the trainer oot twice durin the match.[140]

His next event wis the China Open in Beijing, whaur Murray reached the quarterfinals, losin tae Ivan Ljubičić.[141] Murray then won the Shanghai Rolex Masters dismissin Roger Federer in straucht sets.[142] He did nae drop a single set throughout the event. Murray returned tae Spain tae defend his title at the Valencia Open 500 but lost in the seicont roond tae Juan Mónaco.[143] Houiver, in doubles, Murray pairtnered his brither Jamie Murray tae the final, whaur thay defeatit Mahesh Bhupathi an Max Mirnyi. The victory wis Murray's first doubles title an the seicont time he haed reached a final wi his brither.[144]

Murray reached the quarter finals at the BNP Paribas Masters losin tae Gaël Monfils in three sets. Combined wi his exit an Söderling's takin the title, Murray foond himself pushed doun a spot in the rankings, tae No. 5 frae No. 4.[145] At the Tour finals in London, Murray went 2–1 in roond robin play afore facin Nadal in the semifinal. Thay battled for ower three oors, afore Murray fell tae the Spaniard in a final-set tie-breaker, bringin an end tae his saison.[146]

2011: Second Australian Open final and two Masters titles[eedit | eedit soorce]

Murray an Laura Robson lost in the roond-robin stage 2011 Hopman Cup, losin all three ties even tho Murray won all o his singles matches. Then Murray, alang wi ither stars such as Roger Federer, Rafael Nadal, an Novak Djokovic, participated in the Rally for Relief event tae help raise money for the flood victims in Queensland.[147]

Andy wi his brither Jamie (left) at the 2011 Japan Open

Seedit fift in the 2011 Australian Open, Murray met umwhile champion Novak Djokovic in the final an wis defeatit in straucht sets. In Rotterdam, he wis defeatit bi Marcos Baghdatis in the first roond.[148] Murray reached the semifinals o the doubles tournament wi his brither Jamie. Murray lost tae qualifiers in the first roonds at the Masters Series events in Indian Wells an Miami, efter which he split wi coach Àlex Corretja.[149]

Murray returned tae form at the Monte-Carlo Rolex Masters, but lost tae Nadal in the semifinals.[150] Murray sustained an elbow injury afore the match an subsequently withdrew frae the 2011 Barcelona Open Banco Sabadell due tae the injury.[151] Murray lost in the third roond at the Mutua Madrileña Madrid Open, but made it tae the semifinals o the Rome Masters, whaur he lost tae Novak Djokovic.[152] At the French Open, Murray won twa tough early matches, afore losin in his first semifinal at Roland Garros, against Rafael Nadal.[153][154][155]

Murray defeatit Jo-Wilfried Tsonga tae win his seicont Queen's Club title.[156] At Wimbledon, Murray lost in the semifinal tae Nadal, despite takin the first set.[157] At the Davis Cup tie atween Great Britain an Luxembourg, Murray led the British team tae victory.[158] Murray wis the twa-time defendin 2011 Rogers Cup champion, but lost in the seicont roond tae South African Kevin Anderson.[159] Houiver, the follaein week, he won the 2011 Western & Southern Open, beatin Novak Djokovic, efter Djokovic retired due tae injury.[160] At the 2011 US Open, Murray battled frae twa sets doun tae win a five-set seicont-roond encounter wi Robin Haase, but lost in the semifinals tae Rafael Nadal in fower sets.

Murray easily won the smaw 250-class Thailand Open, an the follaein week he won his third title in fower tournaments at the Rakuten Japan Open Tennis Championships. His opponent in the final wis Rafael Nadal, whom he beat for the first time in the year in three sets. Murray then won the doubles wi his brither Jamie Murray, acomin the first person in the 2011 saison tae capture baith singles an doubles titles at the same tournament. Murray then successfully defended his Shanghai Masters crown wi a straucht-sets victory ower David Ferrer in the final. At the ATP World Tour Finals, Murray lost tae David Ferrer in straucht sets an withdrew frae the tournament efter the loss wi a groin pull. Murray ended the year as No. 4 in the world behind Novak Djokovic, Rafael Nadal, an Roger Federer.

He then beat Thomaz Bellucci in straucht sets but suffered a defeat in the fowert roond tae Kevin Anderson in fower sets. This ended Murray's five-year run o 18 consecutive Grand Slam quarterfinals (nae countin his withdrawal frae the 2013 French Open) syne his third roond loss tae Stan Wawrinka in the 2010 US Open.[274]

Playin against Australie in the semifinals o the Davis Cup World Group in Glesga, Murray won baith his singles rubbers in straucht sets, against Thanasi Kokkinakis an Bernard Tomic.[275] He an aa pairtnered his brither Jamie, an thay won in five sets against the pairin o Sam Groth an Lleyton Hewitt, the results guidin Great Britain tae the Davis Cup final for the first time syne 1978 wi a 3–2 lead ower Australie.[276]

Efter losin in the semifinals o the Shanghai Masters tae Djokovic in straucht sets, Murray reached the finals o the Paris Masters for the lost o juist ane set, wi victories against Borna Ćorić, David Goffin an David Ferrer. Efter a three set win ower Richard Gasquet, he joined Novak Djokovic, Roger Federer an Rafael Nadal as the anly players tae reach the semi-finals (or better) at all nine o the ATP World Tour Masters 1000 tournaments, an an aa ensured that he compiled his best match record in a single saison.[277] He then lost the final tae Djokovic again in straucht sets.

As the world No. 2, Murray participated in the ATP World Tour Finals in London, an wis drawn intae the Ilie Năstase group wi David Ferrer, Rafael Nadal an Stan Wawrinka. He went oot in the roond-robin stage, efter defeatin Ferrer an losin tae Nadal an Wawrinka.[278] Houiver, efter Federer failed tae win the tournament, he finished the saison as world No. 2 for the first time.[279]

In the Davis Cup final, Murray's victory ower Ruben Bemelmans in straucht sets pulled Great Britain level in the final efter Kyle Edmund haed lost the first singles rubber in five sets, played on indoor clay courts at Ghent. He then pairtnered his brither Jamie in a fower-set victory ower the pairin o Steve Darcis an David Goffin, afore defeatin Goffin again in the reverse singles on Sunday, thus ensurin a 3–1 victory for Great Britain, thair first Davis Cup title syne 1936 an thair tenth oweraw.[280] Murray an aa became anly the third person syne the current Davis Cup format wis introduced tae win all aicht o his singles rubbers in a Davis Cup saison, efter John McEnroe an Mats Wilander.[281]

Captain Jeremy Bates surprised everyone bi namin Andy Murray as the British No 1 an Alan Mackin as British No 2. Under the Davis Cup rules, this meant that for Friday’s singles, Murray played the Swiss No 2, Stan Wawrinka while Mackin played the Swiss No 1, Roger Federer. Baith o them lost thair rubbers, giein Switzerland a 2–0 lead. Under the rules, for Sunday’s reverse singles, Bates would hae been able tae substitute Mackin wi Greg Rusedski, sae that Rusedski would play Wawrinka, while Murray played Federer. Houiver, Bates believed that Federer wis virtually unbeatable acause he wis on a winnin streak an hadn’t lost syne Juin,[286] an consequently Britain gambled on beatin Wawrinka twice, wi Murray playin Wawrinka when he wis freshest. In the doubles, Murray/Rusedski played Federer an Yves Allegro, leavin Rusedski tae play anly ance.[287][288] The British tactics came tae nought as Switzerland won the doubles rubber as well, gainin un unassailable 3–0 lead efter twa the days. Murray vs. Djokovic[eedit | eedit soorce]

Main article: Djokovic–Murray rivalry

Novak Djokovic an Andy Murray hae met 34 times wi Djokovic leadin 24–10.[328][329] Djokovic leads 5–1 on clay, 19–7 on haurd courts, an Murray leads 2–0 on gress. The twa are awmaist exactly the same age, wi Murray bein anly a week aulder than Djokovic. Thay went tae trainin camp thegither, an Murray won the first match thay ever played as teenagers. The pair hae met 16 times in finals, wi Djokovic leadin 9–7. Ten o the finals war at ATP Masters 1000 events, an thay are tied at 5–5. Thay hae met in seiven Major finals: The 2011 Australian Open, the 2012 US Open, the 2013 Australian Open, the 2013 Wimbledon Championships, the 2015 Australian Open, the 2016 Australian Open, an the 2016 French Open. Djokovic haes won in Australie fower times an thair single French open final, Murray emerged as the victor at the US Open an Wimbledon. The umwhile o Murray's victories wis the langest ever final at the US Open, tyin wi the 1988 final played atween Ivan Lendl an Mats Wilander at 4 oors an 53 minutes, whilst the latter wis notable for bein the first home triumph in men's singles at Wimbledon syne 1936.

Thay an aa played a nearly five-oor lang semifinal match in the 2012 Australian Open, which Djokovic won 7–5 in the fift set efter Murray led 2 sets tae 1. Murray an Djokovic met again in 2012 at the London 2012 Olympic Gemmes, wi Murray winnin in straucht sets. Durin the final o the 2012 Shanghai Masters, Murray held five championship pynts in the seicont set, houiver Djokovic saved each o them, forcin a decidin set. He eventually prevailed tae win his first Shanghai Masters title, endin Murray's 12–0 winnin streak at the event. The three set matches thay played in Rome an Shanghai in 2011 an 2012 respectively war voted the ATP World Tour Match o the Year for each respective saison.[330][331] Due tae the tight competeetion atween 2008 an 2013, mony saw this as the emergin rivalry, as the twa are baith in thair prime years, an are baith likely tae be playin for at least anither five years.[332][333] Houiver, Djokovic went on tae dominate the rivalry efter the 2013 Wimbledon final, winnin 8 matches in a row, until Murray ended the streak in the final o the 2015 Rogers Cup.

Murray vs. Federer[eedit | eedit soorce]

Main article: Federer–Murray rivalry

Andy Murray an Roger Federer hae met 25 times wi Federer leadin 14–11. Federer leads 12–10 on haurd courts, 2–1 on gress, an thay hae never met on clay. Efter Federer won the first professional match thay played, Murray dominated the rivalry frae 2006 until 2014 wi Federer beatin Murray three straucht times tae reverse the lead at the 2014 ATP World Tour Finals.[334] Federer leads 5–3 in finals, haein won each o thair Grand Slam Final meetins at the 2008 US Open[105] an 2010 Australian Open, baith o which Federer won in straucht sets, an the 2012 Wimbledon Championships, whaur Murray teuk the first set, but ended up losin in 4 sets. Murray leads 6–3 in ATP 1000 tournaments an 2–0 in finals. Thay hae met five times at the ATP World Tour Finals, wi Murray winnin in Shanghai in 2008[335] an Federer comin oot victorious in London in 2009, 2010, 2012, an in 2014.

In August 2012, Murray met Federer in the final of the London 2012 Olympics at Wimbledon Centre Court, juist fower weeks efter the 2012 Wimbledon Final, in which Federer haed defeatit Murray tae win his record-tyin 7t title at the All-England Club. Murray defeatit Federer in straucht sets tae win the gowd medal, denyin Federer a Career Golden Slam. In 2013 Murray beat Federer for the first time in a Major in the semi-finals o the Australian Open, prevailin in five sets efter Federer haed come back twice frae a set doun.[336] Thair last grand slam meetin wis at the 2015 Wimbledon Championships semifinals, whaur a dominant Federer defeatit Murray in straucht sets, earnin a place in his 10t Wimbledon final. Murray is ane o anly three players tae hae recorded 10 or more victories ower Federer, the ither twa bein Nadal an Djokovic. Thair maist recent meetin teuk place at the 2015 Cincinnati Masters semifinals, wi Federer winnin the match in twa close sets, recordin his fift consecutive victory ower Murray.

Murray vs. Nadal[eedit | eedit soorce]

Main article: Murray–Nadal rivalry

Andy Murray haes played against Rafael Nadal on 24 occasions syne 2007, wi Nadal leadin 17–7. Nadal leads 7–2 on clay, 3–0 on gress an 7–5 on haurd courts. The pair regularly meet at Grand Slam level, wi nine oot o thair twenty-ane meetins comin in slams, wi Nadal leadin 7–2 (3–0 at Wimbledon, 2–0 at the French Open, 1–1 at the Australian Open an 1–1 at the US Open).[337] Aicht o these nine appearances hae been at Quarter-final an Semi-final level. Thay hae never met in a Slam final, houiver, Murray leads 3–1 in ATP finals, wi Nadal winnin at Indian Wells in 2009[338] an Murray winnin in Rotterdam the same year,[339] Tokyo[340] in 2011, an at Madrid in 2015.

Murray lost three consecutive Grand Slam semifinals tae Nadal in 2011 frae the French Open tae the US Open. Of the past 20 Grand Slam drawsheets, thay hae been drawn in the same half 16 times. The pair haed nae met for three years syne the final o the 2011 Japan Open until the quarter finals o the 2014 Rome Masters, awtho thay war scheduled tae meet in the semi-final o the 2012 Miami Masters afore Nadal withdrew wi injury.[341] At the semifinal stage o the 2014 French Open, Nadal triumphed in a dominant straucht sets win for the loss o juist 6 gemmes. In ane o thair maist recent meetins, Murray beat Nadal for the first time on clay,[342] an the first time in a Masters 1000 final,[citation needit] at the Madrid Open in 2015. Murray continued his form tae beat Nadal at the 2016 Mutual Madrid Open in the semifinals.[citation needit]

Playing style[eedit | eedit soorce]

At the Boodles Challenge, Stoke Pairk, Juin 2012

Murray plays an all-court gemme wi an emphasis on defensive baseline play, an professional tennis coach Paul Annacone stated that Murray "may be the best counterpuncher on tour today."[343][344] His strengths include groondstrokes wi law error rate, the ability tae anticipate an react, an his transition frae defence tae offence wi speed, which enables him tae hit winners frae defensive poseetions. His playin style haes been likened tae that o Miloslav Mečíř. Murray an aa haes ane o the best twa-handed backhands on the tour, wi dynamic stroke execution[345] while he primarily uses his forehaund an a sliced backhaund tae let opponents play intae his haunds.[346] Tim Henman haes stated that Murray mey nou hae the best lob in the gemme, succeedin Lleyton Hewitt.[347] In addition tae this, Murray is a good volleyer, occasionally approachin the net, an executes a fine drop shot, awtho his drop shots are whiles a drawback against faster players lik Roger Federer.[348][349]

Murray's tactics uisually involve passive exchanges frae the baseline. He is capable o injectin sudden pace intae his groondstrokes tae surprise his opponents who are uised tae the slow rally. Murray is an aa ane o the tap returners in the gemme, eften able tae block back fast serves wi his excellent reach an ability tae anticipate. For this reason, Murray is rarely aced.[350] Murray is kent for bein ane o the maist intelligent tacticians on the court, eften biggin pynts.[351][352] Murray is maist proficient on a fast surface,[344] lik gress, whaur he haes won sax singles titles includin the Wimbledon Championships an the 2012 Olympic Gowd Medal. He haes worked haurd syne 2008 on improvin his clay court gemme,[353] ultimately winnin his first clay titles durin 2015 at Munich an Madrid, as well as his first French Open final. While Murray's serve is a major weapon for him, wi his first serve reachin speeds o 130 mph or higher on some occasions,[354] it whiles can be a weakness for him when hit under pressure,[355] especially wi a more inconsistent seicont serve. Syne his 2011 saison an Ivan Lendl's coaching, he haes worked tae improve his consistency, seicont serve, returns an mental gemme, resultin in a more offensive gemme.[354][356][357]

National identity[eedit | eedit soorce]

Murray identifies himself as Scottish an British.[372] His national identity haes eften been commented on bi the media.[373] Whilst makin a cameo appearance on comedy shaw Outnumbered, Murray wis asked whether he wis British or Scottish, tae which he responded "It depends if I'm winning".[374] Much o the discussion aboot Murray's national identity began prior tae Wimbledon 2006, when he wis quoted as sayin he would "support whoever England is playing" at the 2006 World Cup. Inglis ex-tennis player Tim Henman confirmed that the remarks haed been made in jest an war anly in response tae Murray bein teased bi jurnalist Des Kelly an Henman aboot Scotland's failure tae qualify.[375]

Murray initially refused tae endorse either side o the debate in the 2014 referendum on Scottish independence, citin the abuse he haed received efter his comments in 2006.[376] Juist afore the referendum, Murray tweeted a message that wis considered bi the media tae be supportive o independence.[lower-alpha 1][377][378][379] He received online abuse for expressin his opinion, includin messages that war describit as "vile" bi Police Scotland; ane referred tae the Dunblane massacre.[379] A few days efter the vote, in which a 55% majority opposed Scottish independence, Murray said that he did nae regret statin his view, but admitted that it wis oot o character an that he would concentrate on his tennis career in the future.[379]
