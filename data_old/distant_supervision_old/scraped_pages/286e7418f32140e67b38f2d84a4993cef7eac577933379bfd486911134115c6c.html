    Microsoft Surface 2.0 wins PopSci’s Best of What’s New

    Posted November 16, 2011 July 9, 2014 By Steve Clayton - Editor
    • 0

    I’ll be spending some time with the creators of Surface 2.0 today so it was fitting to read that the Samsung SUR40 for Microsoft Surface has been awarded an  “Best of What’s New” Award for 2011 from Popular Science magazine.

    You may be familiar with Surface 1.0 – the big table. Things have changed quite a lot with Surface 2.0 as the video above shows – key to this is a technology called PixelSense.

    Read more about Surface on Next at Microsoft

    [update] hot on the heels of this news, the Surface team announced today that the Samsung SUR40 is now available for pre-order. Find out more at News Center.
