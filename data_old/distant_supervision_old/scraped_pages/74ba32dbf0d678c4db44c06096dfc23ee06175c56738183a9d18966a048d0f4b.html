UK National Racketball Champs 2016
08-11 Jul, Edgbaston Priory, Birmingham
Racketball Champions crowned in Birmingham

Vail and Lawrence take the titles

Tim Vail won his 7th National Racketball title beating a very tired Ted Jeal 3-0. Jeal who was heavily fatigued from his semi-final match against Mike Harris this morning was no match for the stylish Tim Vail.

In the Women’s event Natalie Lawrence, who earlier had won the Over 35 championship, completed the double when she beat Chloe Marshall 3-0 to take the Women’s Championship.

Mike Edwards (Tournament Director) said: “A massive thank you to everyone who took part in the National Racketball Championships. The atmosphere around the club was fantastic and on court the matches were well fought all weekend.”
 

What an incredible day of racketball on the final day of the National Championships.

The day began with 6 times National Men’s Champion Team Vail easing his way in to the final with a 3-0 win over Luke Bishop, who was still weary after his mammoth win the night before. Vail’s opponent in the final is Ted Jeal who beat Mike Harris after he retired in the 5th due to a slight hamstring injury!

In the Women’s Semi-Final Chloe Marshall (Oxfordshire) continued her good form by defeating Debbie Hale (Dorset) 3-0. Marshall’s opponent in the Final is Natalie Lawrence who won a hard fought battle against Rachel Woolford 3-1.
