  2. Allen Christian Church Historic Marker Installation

    October 31, 2015, 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM @ Allen Christian Church

    Allen Christian Church Historic Marker Installation2015-10-31T14:00:00

    The Allen Heritage Guild will install a marker at the historic Allen Christian Church. Mayor Terrell will issue a proclamation and Tom Keener will discuss the importance of the church to Allen’s history. The Allen Heritage Guild restored this prairie style Allen Christian Church to its original majesty in 2008. Built in 1918, the Allen Christian Church contains beautiful stain glass windows that feature the names of early pioneer families and is a centerpiece of the Historic Village. Descendants of families identified in the windows will also participate in the ceremony.

    Allen Christian Church450 E. St. MaryAllenTX75002

  1. APL Pet Festival and Costume Contest

    October 31, 2015, 2:30 PM @ Library Courtyard (or Auditorium in case of bad weather)

    APL Pet Festival and Costume Contest2015-10-31T14:30:00

    Bring the family and four-legged friends to Allen Public Library's first pet costume contest and festival. Dress up your pet in costume and compete for Best Costume and Owner/Pet Lookalike. Polish up on your pet's tricks for the Best Trick Contest. Prizes will be awarded for the winners of each contest. Entry fee for the contest is an item(s) to donate to the Allen Animal Shelter. Some items the shelter needs include: Purina/Pedigree dog/cat food, collars, leashes, laundry detergent, and chew toys. The Allen Animal Shelter will be in attendance if you are looking to add a furry member to your family or get more information on how to help the animals awaiting adoption at the shelter. All ages.

    Library Courtyard (or Auditorium in case of bad weather)300 N. Allen DriveAllenTX75013
