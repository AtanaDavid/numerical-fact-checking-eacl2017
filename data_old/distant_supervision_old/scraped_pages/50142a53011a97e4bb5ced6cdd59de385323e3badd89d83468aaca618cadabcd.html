Lot Details
A collection of 1994 Bell Atlantic Classic score cards signed by Ryder Cup players
To include Arnold Palmer, Jerry Barber, Dow Finsterwald, Doug Ford, Gene Littler and Miller Barber. (5)

Footnotes

  • Arnold Palmer made his Ryder Cup debut on the 1961 team. This was the first year that the Ryder Cup was expanded to a 3 day event. In 1961 Jerry Barber was the playing Captain for the United States. At the 1994 Bell Atlantic Classic, five members of that winning team were in the field. This lot contains a tournament Scorecard from each of those players and additionally, there is a marker signature from 1969 & 1971 Ryder Cup participant Miller Barber.
