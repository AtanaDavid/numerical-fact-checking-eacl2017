Life expectancy improves in United States

In 2014 the life expectancy in United States increased to 78.94 years.

That year, the life expectancy for women was 81.40 years and for men 76.60 years.

United States’s position was held this year in position 36th with respect to the ranking of 191 we publish life expectancy.

If we look at the change in life expectancy in United States over the past several years, we find that it is higher than in 2013, when it was 78.84 years, which is the same of what happens compared to 2004, when it was 77.49 years.
