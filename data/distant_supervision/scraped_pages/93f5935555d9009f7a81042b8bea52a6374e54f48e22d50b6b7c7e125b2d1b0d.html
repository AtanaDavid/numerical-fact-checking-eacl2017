Definition

The health care profession comprises all persons who provide services aiming at the preservation or improvement of the health of individuals or the treatment or care of individuals who are injured, sick or disabled. In aÂ very broad definition, all individuals that participate in some way in the delivery of health care are part of the health care profession.
