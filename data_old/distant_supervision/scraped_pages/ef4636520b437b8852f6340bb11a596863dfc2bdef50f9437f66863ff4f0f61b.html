Palo Alto Training


Experience the official Palo Alto curriculum with Fast Lane. Palo Alto Networks Training delivers you the knowledge and expertise necessary to effectively manage the next-generation firewall and ensure your business is secure from dangerous threats. Through lecture and hands-on labs, you will learn the skills needed to configure, support, troubleshoot and manage Palo Alto Networks next generation firewalls to secure your network and safely enable applications.

Palo Alto Networks Firewall Courses:

Palo Alto Networks Firewall Training

  • Firewall: Install, Configure, and Manage (201)
  • Firewall: Configure Extended Features (205)
  • Panorama: Manage Multiple Firewalls (221)
  • Firewall: Manage Cyberthreats (231)
  • Firewall: Debug and Troubleshoot (311)
