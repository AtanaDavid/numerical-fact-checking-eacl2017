Job: Lead Event Attendant

This posting has expired and is no longer available.

Browse Similar Jobs: Government Customer Service

Jobing Description

Overall Job Objective

The purpose of this position provide assistance in the operations of guest services at the Allen Event Center.  This is accomplished by assisting and greeting customers, answering questions via telephone and in person, providing direction to ushers, collecting tickets and directing patrons to assigned seat locations.  Other duties may include assisting patrons with general facility information, assisting the guest services supervisor, and providing direction to security staff, to parking staff, and other third party staff.  This position may provide lead direction to other employees.
 
 Essential Functions:
The information listed below is intended to describe the general nature and level of work being performed by individuals assigned to this position. They are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all responsibilities, duties and skills required of this position.
 
  • Assists with the coordination of event attendants by assigning daily work activities, scheduling staff, modifying work schedules, training and coaching employees, and provides feedback to correct minor performance issues.
     
  • Ensures patrons do not violate facility rules and regulations.
     
  • Collects and scans event tickets for event attendance and assists guests with locating their seats.
     
  • Perform general administrative duties such as answering phones and greeting guests and employees in a manner which will promote the highest level of internal and external customer service.
     
  • Monitors CCTV, control systems, and visitors. Reports irregularities.
     
  • Maintains and updates records and files including event activity log and guest comment log.
     
  • Assists with escalated customer issues.
     
  • Assists with planning, supervision and coordination of event needs including event staffing logistics, promoter relations, administration, guest services and event security.
     
  • Assist with guest services event plan execution by issuing equipment and communicating event details and irregularities to event attendants.
  • Ensure building is opened and secured by unlocking and locking doors, monitoring patron, staff, and third party activity, receiving deliveries, and clearing the building prior to securing it.

 Job Requirements:
Formal Education / Knowledge
Work requires knowledge necessary to understand basic operational, technical, or office processes. Level of knowledge equivalent to four years of high school or equivalency.

Experience
One year experience required. Must be at least eighteen years of age.

Certifications and Other Requirements
This position requires the ability to work a flexible schedule that includes working evenings, nights, weekends and some holidays.
