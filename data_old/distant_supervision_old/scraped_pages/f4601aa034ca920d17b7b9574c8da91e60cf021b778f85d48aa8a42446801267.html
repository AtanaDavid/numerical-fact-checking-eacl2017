Northumberland Cricket Board Limited
18 October at 04:18 ·

The details for this year’s ECB National Indoor Hardball Competition 2016 are as follows:-

There will be 3/4 groups of 3 teams depending on how many teams enter, with each group playing all of their games between 4pm and 7pm on one Sunday, then a finals day in December with all of the group winners. The overall Northumberland winners will then go onto play in the Northern Regional Final, with the winners from that day going onto Lords for the National Final.

Dates are:-...
- Group 1 fixtures played on Sunday 13th November
- Group 2 on Sunday 20th November
- Group 3 Sunday 27th November
- Group 4 (if needed) Sunday 4th December
- Finals Day Sunday 11th December

Spots in the groups will be allocated on a first come first served basis. If clubs would prefer a specific group/date, then please let us know and efforts will be made to accommodate.

The deadline for entries is Monday 31st October.

For more information, or to enter a team, please contact James Morgan at james.morgan@northumberlandcricketboard.co.uk

11 October at 03:09 · Hexham ·

After two weeks of the Haydon Bridge Indoor League new participants Humshaugh Cricket Club top the table with a 100% record. Week 2 kicked off with the returning Haltwhistle score 147-11 from their 12 overs, in reply Matfen Hall Cricket Club finished on 188-8 to record a 41 run win. Game 2 saw the tightest game of the evening, batting first Tynedale Cricket Club scored 165-7. In reply Haydonians finished on 167-5 to record a two run victory. The final game of the evening saw Humshaugh post an impressive 202-6 from their 12 overs, in reply Stocksfield Cricket Club gave it a go but lost too many wickets finishing on 161-13.
