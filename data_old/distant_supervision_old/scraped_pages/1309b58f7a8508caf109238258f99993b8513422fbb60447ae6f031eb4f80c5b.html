WGA Highlights



The Western Golf Association was founded in 1899 to promote golf across the west. With the help of famed amateur golfer Charles "Chick" Evans, the WGA created the Evans Scholars Foundation and funded the first two Evans Scholarships in 1930.


More than 10,000 caddies have graduated as Evans Scholars. This fall, 910 Evans Scholars attended 19 universities coast to coast. They have a cumulative GPA of 3.2 and graduate at a rate of 95 percent. The average value of an Evans Scholarship is $80,000.


Since 2007, the BMW Championship has donated more than $21 million to the Evans Scholars Foundation. That funding helps provide Evans Scholarships to hundreds of deserving, hard-working caddies who qualify for this life-changing gift.
