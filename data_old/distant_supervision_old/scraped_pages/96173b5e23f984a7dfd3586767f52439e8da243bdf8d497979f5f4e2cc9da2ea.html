Reigning NBA MVP Stephen Curry and likely next NFL MVP Cam Newton have become friends over the former’s fandom of the Carolina Panthers.

Curry and Newton will turn adversaries in at least one Las Vegas sports book next weekend. The Golden State Warriors superstar is a 4.5-point favorite to score more in his game against the Oklahoma City Thunder on Saturday, Feb. 6, than the Panthers will the next day against the Denver Broncos in Super Bowl 50.

That’s just one of the hundreds of Super Bowl proposition wagers the Westgate Las Vegas Superbook posted to its betting board Thursday night.

The options range from the straight-forward — over/under 232.5 passing yards for Newton — to the outrageous — a near pick’em for which team will commit the first penalty. The conversation-starting cross-sport props top it all off.
