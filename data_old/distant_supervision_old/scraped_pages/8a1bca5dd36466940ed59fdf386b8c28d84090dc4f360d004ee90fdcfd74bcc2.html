      Pennsylvania Farm Show is a 9 day event being held from 6th January to 14th January 2017 at the PA State Farm Show Complex & Expo Center in Harrisburg, United States Of America. This event showcases products like agriculture product , energy equipment, animal health products, birds food, chemicals, dairy equipments etc. in the Agriculture & Forestry industry.

      Exhibitor Profile

      Various exhibitors are looking forward to participate in the Pennsylvania Farm Show from different parts of the country as well as from other countries itself. Lots of renowned personalities from the world of agriculture industry will be sharing their experiences to the visitors. The exhibitors will be showcasing the many ways in which agriculture is keeping Pennsylvania growing. The exhibitors will be professionals, importers and buyers of agriculture industry.
