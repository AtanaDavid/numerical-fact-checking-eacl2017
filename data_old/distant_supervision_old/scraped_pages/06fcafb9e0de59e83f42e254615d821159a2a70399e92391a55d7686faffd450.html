Queens Park Rangers

Queens Park Rangers Football Club play at the Loftus Road ground under Mark Hughes. Football.co.uk presents the best resource for the Premier League club including the latest Queens Park Rangers football news, stats, fixtures and results. They were formed in 1886 when a team known as St Judes (which was formed in 1882) merged with a team named the Christchurch Rangers. The resulting team was called Queens Park Rangers, because most of the players came from Queens Park. Queens Park Rangers football club won the League Cup in 1967.
