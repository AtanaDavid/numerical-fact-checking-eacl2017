“People pleasing park”
Reviewed 9 March 2016

This is the home of the Allen USA Celebration fireworks and concert festival, typically held the weekend prior to Independence Day. Attendance at this gargantuan event is around 70K with one of the largest fireworks exhibitions in N. Texas. Beyond that, Celebration park hosts numerous soccer, tennis and baseball games. It also has a splash park and a lengthy walking trail.

Visited June 2015

“play time”
Reviewed 18 February 2016

This is a great family park. It has a play area (jungle jim) that is great for kids of all areas. It is handicap accessible. In the summer, it has a sprinkler park. Kids especially little ones love it. If you like to walk it has a paved walking/biking path around the whole park. There are shaded areas and picnic tables. There are Bathrooms.

“Day in the Park”
Reviewed 2 February 2016

Great place for the family. There is a small water spray park for toddlers to maybe pre-teens, a play area with swings and slides, soccer fields, basketball court, walking/jogging trail and more. It is a great place to have a picnic, get some exercise or just relax. The annual Independence Day celebration is always nice. Allen usually has it early so that it is not in competition with other celebrations.

“Fantastic all around park for all members of the family.”
Reviewed 15 December 2015

Celebration park is the site of Allen's annual Fourth of July event. This park has everything you need to become active. Soccer, baseball and tennis facilities are utilized nightly. The back side of the park holds a large variety of children equipment from climbing bars to various sizes of slides for the kids. A dancing water park is open during the summertime. Centrally located in Allen, operated by the parks department.

Visited September 2015
