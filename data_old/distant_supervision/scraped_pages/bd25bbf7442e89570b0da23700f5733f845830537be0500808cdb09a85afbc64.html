About “Fade Away”

https://twitter.com/Logic301/status/662060635493040128

The song re-introduces listeners to Thalia from his Under Pressure album, and features a cameo from Big Sean.

Logic also sat with Genius to explain the song:

“Fade Away” is about accepting death and this is just a personal opinion that no matter who you are, what historical figure, any of that, one day your name will be forgotten. Whether a comet hits the Earth or we all repopularize one day, you’ll be forgotten. Do something for you before you fade away.
