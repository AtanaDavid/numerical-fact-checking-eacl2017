Nicola Adams wins gold at Feliks Stamm

Posted by tonyphillips on 11:13 pm in News | 0 comments

Haringey’s talented Oympic champion Nicola Adams claimed another gold medal this weekend in the final of the Feliks Stamm tournament in Warsaw, Poland. There was no sign of ring rust as the Leeds boxer, who competes for Haringey when she’s not training at GB Boxing, returned to competition for her first international tournament since she secured gold in the Women’s EU Championships last year. A dominating performance in the semi finals saw Nicola untroubled by Stoyka Petrova – a Bulgarian she has already beaten five times, while the final saw her beat Russian Saiana Sagataeva to claim gold. GB coach Bob Dillon said: “Nicola was up against a very tough Russian opponent in the final. It was a hard fought win, but as a boxer she’s a class above and she deserved to take the gold medal.” Feliks Stamm featured boxers from: Belarus, Ukraine, Hungary, France, Russia, Ireland, England, Belgium, Bulgaria, Greece, New Zealand, Spain, Tajikistan, Kazakhstan, Qatar, Armenia, Georgia, Canada, China, USA, Mexico and Brazil. Haringey’s Dinner Show a great success

Posted by tonyphillips on 10:32 pm in News | 0 comments

Haringey Police Community Boxing club’s 14th Annual Dinner show (28th March 2014) was a great success. It was a spectacular evening of boxing entertainment, coupled with the refined sounds of a string quartet and showcased in the elegant and newly refurbished Decorium Banqueting Suite in Wood Green. Chief Superintendent Victor Olisa, Haringey’s Borough Commander, enjoyed the evening’s entertainment and presented prizes to competing boxers. Oriance wins her first 4×2 bout, is awarded the Tommy Payne Best Haringey Boxer of the night trophy and gets the Jeff Crawford memorial for Best Haringey club person throughout the season. Orio does the triple in her first full season with Haringey, she is now in preparation for an all female tournament, showcased during this years male elite championships up in Liverpool (April 26th).
