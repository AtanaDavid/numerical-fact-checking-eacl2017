About the Race

The Rock ‘n’ Roll Liverpool Marathon & 1/2 returns 27th-28th May. There’s loads to love in 2017 – a Saturday 5K,  2-day Expo and the main events on Sunday. It’s a happening you don’t want to miss. Take part in Liverpool’s Summer of Love. More Music, More Medals, More Fun!

Learn More

Fantastic Routes

The marathon and half marathon courses show off the best of Liverpool. Race by the new Beatles statue, the historic Liver Building and Matthew Street, run down Penny Lane and through colorful Chinatown. All races start at Albert Docks and finish outside Echo Arena. Check out the routes.
