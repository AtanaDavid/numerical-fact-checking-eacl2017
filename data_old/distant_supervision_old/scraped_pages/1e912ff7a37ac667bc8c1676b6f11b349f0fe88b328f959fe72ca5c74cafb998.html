Profile & History

David's big hobby is also a business - he owns a farm near Crewe in Staffordshire where his father hellps to look after his prized herd of Hereford cattle.

"Farming is long hours for little money," he says. "Golf is short hours for lots of money."
Europe's joint top scorer in 1995 Ryder Cup where he won points with Seve Ballesteros and Bernhard Langer as partners then beat Brad Faxon in singles.
