FIT Surface Design Competition Runner-Up

Jun 18, 2015
735
SHARE
Facebook

Honey Jernquist designed the rug as part of a Surface Design competition at The Fashion Institute of Technology.

Mike Marcy, Noreen Seabrook Marketing made the rug in Nepal, organized the exhibition at Patterson, Flynn, and Martin in NYC, underwrote the cash prizes to the two winners and two runners-up, and then auctioned the rugs with proceeds going to The Alpha Workshops Studio School, also in NYC.
