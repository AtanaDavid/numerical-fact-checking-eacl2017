England
NASUWT members in England can get support, advice and information on employment-related issues by contacting the Member Support Advice Team. The team is available from 8am to 6.30pm Monday to Friday. 

NASUWT Representatives with enquiries should contact their Regional Centre.

Members in England only can call 03330 145550 free in any inclusive minute package from a landline or mobile phone. This line is for advice issues only.

Members in Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland should contact their National Centre.

Email: advice@mail.nasuwt.org.uk (Members in England only)
General Enquiries: nasuwt@mail.nasuwt.org.uk
