OUTLOOK 2017: INVESTING AMID POLITICAL UNCERTAINTY

Political uncertainty, rising populism and policy divergence will continue to dominate in 2017. Interest rates have moved away from emergency settings in the US and are likely to continue along a hiking path, but monetary policy in Europe and Japan will remain very accommodative. Sharp changes in politics suggest that an easy monetary policy stance is likely to remain commonplace while the US pulls away with President Trump at the helm. Read the 2017 market outlook from Mark Burgess, Chief Investment Officer, Europe, Middle East & Africa (EMEA)and global head of equities.
