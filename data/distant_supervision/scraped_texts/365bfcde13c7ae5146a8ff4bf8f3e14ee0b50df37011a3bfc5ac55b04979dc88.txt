On 31 March 2016 Capital Group Global Absolute Income Grower (LUX) (CGGAIGLU) absorbed the sub-fund with the same name in the Capital International Portfolios 2 (CIP2) umbrella. CIP2 was consequently dissolved with effect on the same day. Copies of the latest audited annual and unaudited semi-annual reports of CIP2 may be obtained upon request, free of charge, at the registered offices of Capital International Fund [6C, route de Trèves, L–2633 Senningerberg, Grand-Duché de Luxembourg].
