Telugu Association of Scotland

TAS is a Registered Charity organisation (No SC039542). We are a cultural, community services based, non-profit and non-religious organisation abiding under laws of Scotland.

Why Join Us?

TAS celebrates festivals, occasions and conducts sports and activities for children, women and men throughout the year.

  • TAS is a Registerd Charity organisation (No SC039542).
  • Provides opportunities to meet Telugu people living in and around Scotland.
  • TAS is run voluntarily by people of all ages and backgrounds.
  • Become an ambassador of our great Telugu Tradition.
  • Be a part of TAS family with an intention to contribute to the community.

About Us

Telugu association of Scotland (TAS) is a non-profit Telugu charity organization in Scotland. TAS was formed in 2001 with a clear vision and objective to lay foundations for Telugu speaking community.
