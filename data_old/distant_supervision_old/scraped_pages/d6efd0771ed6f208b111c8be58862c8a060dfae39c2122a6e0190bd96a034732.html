Janko Tipsarević (Serbie Cyrillic: Јанко Типсаревић, Serbian pronunciation: [jâːŋkɔ tipsǎːrɛʋit͡ɕ]; born Juin 22, 1984) is a Serbie professional tennis player who is currently ranked Warld No. 10 as o Aprile 1, 2013. His career-heich singles rankin is Warld No. 8, achieved on Aprile 2, 2012. In his career, he haes wan fower ATP Warld Tour titles, twa Futures, an nine toornaments in the ATP Challenger Series. Tipsarević an aa wan the 2001 Australian Open Junior title.

Tipsarević haes achieved twa victories ower a Warld No. 1 player, havin defeatit compatriot Novak Djokovic twice. He an aa haes thirteen victories against ither players in the tap 10: fower wins against Tomáš Berdych, an ane each against Fernando González, Mikhail Youzhny, David Ferrer, James Blake, Gaël Monfils, Andy Murray an Juan Mónaco. He haulds victories ower umwhile Warld No. 1 players Carlos Moyá, Marat Safin, Lleyton Hewitt, Juan Carlos Ferrero & Andy Roddick. His best results at a Grand Slam hae been reachin the quarterfinals at the US Open in 2011 & 2012.
