Help:

Use the Education UK search tool to find courses and scholarships at UK schools, colleges and universities. Select an option:
  • Courses:
    • Learn English: English language courses
    • Pre-university academic (e.g. A-levels, IB): Academic courses for students aged 16 and over, prior to higher education. Includes A-levels, International Baccalaureate, Scottish Highers, BTEC Firsts and Nationals
    • Vocational and technical (e.g. NVQ, BTEC, VQ): Practical, work-related and career-focused courses for students aged 16 and over
    • Pathways to higher education: Courses which lead to further study at university level, including HNDs, foundation and access courses
    • Undergraduate (e.g. Bachelor's degree): Higher education courses including bachelors’ degrees (e.g. BA, BSc, BEng), foundation degrees, HNDs and DipHEs
    • Postgraduate (e.g. Master's degree, PGCE, PhD): Higher education courses including masters’ degrees (e.g. MA, MSc), PhDs/doctorates and professional diplomas
    • MBA: MBA (Master of Business Administration) are postgraduate courses designed for a career in business and management
  • Scholarships: Find scholarships, grants and bursaries to study on a UK course
  • Uni/college/school: Find a school, college or university by name; or browse boarding schools (residential schools for students up to age 18)
  • Articles: Find information, advice or features on all aspects of UK student life.
