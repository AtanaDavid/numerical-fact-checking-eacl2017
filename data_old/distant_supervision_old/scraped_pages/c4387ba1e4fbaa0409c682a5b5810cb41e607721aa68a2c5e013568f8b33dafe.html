Runner Up

I'm not one for second place
In life or love or existence as a whole.
If I'm not going to win
The game seems impossibly dull.

I don't care.

In second place, silver trophy in hand
I don't care to speak to you.
I don't care about your issues; I have my own.
And every word you speak is still cruel.

But I can't be hurt when I don't care
And pretending's never been a hardship.
But come the end of this year
I'll rescind back into the behavior of a hermit.

I'm not one to be thought of as a runner up.
I've loved and lost my motivation to speak.
I'm not looking for a new reason.
It's first place or no place for me.

I think this might be the first shitty poem I've written. Like, legitimately shitty.

Runner up

I flick through dog eared memory
each one high on LSD
and every memory wants to be
forefront in the gallery.
The winning post,the thinning host of
some achievements I'll not boast but
some,
yes some,
came unbidden on the morning sun
loading chambers in the midnight gun
to run like mercury,
but memory,by design for some and
like the bullets in the midnight gun
kills me
in the end.

Runner-Up

You are the best.
I am right behind you.
I try my hardest,
But with you,
The best title
I can claim
Is "also-ran".
You deserve
Every bit
Of what you win.
Yet, I still feel remorse,
Climbing to
Not-quite-the-top.
This is for you,
Number one,
So you know
What it's like;
Living life as
Your shadow.

Dedicated to the best of the best, the one who subconsciously wrote my biography with his left hand.

shoot the runner-up.

Every tear you cry
Is another blinded eye
Turning back towards the sun
As they kiss the world goodbye
Every time you run
Down the barrels of their guns
Saints are rolling in their graves
While the killers have their fun
And mothers of the world
Weep as flags become unfurled
Over all the caskets of the sons they bore
Someone go and tell Miss Babylon
I think we've found our whore
And it makes myself hurt when I call you out
You'll admit they suffer but
You won't say much about it...
We set our sights up on the stars in model roles
While everything just falls apart
Because you won't lift a finger yourself
You're a slave to your wealth
And you won't get inside
Til you swallow your pride
Yeah.
We line the cage in paper while they roll in gold
The world's about to find out
Who we've all been sold to
So open your eyes
And start changing your minds
Cus you won't get inside
Til you swallow your pride
Yeah.
There'll be no peace
Til the poor man is fed
And there'll be no peace
Til the harlots are wed
And there'll be no peace
Til the war machine is dead
And there'll be no peace
Til the liars are all fed to the lions.
