Average monthly temperature in the U.S. from January 2013 to July 2016 (in Fahrenheit)

This statistic exhibits the average monthly temperatures in the United States between January 2013 and July 2016 in Fahrenheit. In April 2013, the average temperature was 49.68 degrees Fahrenheit. The average temperature in 2015 was 2.4 degrees Fahrenheit above the 20th century average and was the second warmest year on record.
