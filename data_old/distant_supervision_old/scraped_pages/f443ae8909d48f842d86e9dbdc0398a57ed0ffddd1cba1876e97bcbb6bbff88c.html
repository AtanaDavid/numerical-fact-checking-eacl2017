win·ner

 (wĭn′ər)
n.
One that wins, especially a victor in sports or a notably successful person.

winner

(ˈwɪnə)
n
1. a person or thing that wins
2. informal a person or thing that seems sure to win or succeed

win•ner

(ˈwɪn ər)

n.
a person or thing that wins; victor.
[1325–75]
