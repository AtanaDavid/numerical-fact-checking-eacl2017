3 June 2015 ·

Today, part of the AADC team went to MPI - Gaming Teaching and Research Center to meet with a group of Volunteers that participated at AADC 2015.

It was a fruitful and pleasant exchange of ideas, where Volunteers gave their feedback about participating on the gaming operations, what they have learned from the experience and shared suggestions for further editions.

The platform served to give to each Volunteer their well deserved Certificate of Participation and Recognition Le...tter, as this experience was considered much valuable for their future careers. In some cases, AADC was the first "live casino" experience as it replicated real gaming practices that will be part of their promising future jobs.

AADC deeply thanks to MPI - Gaming Teaching and Research Center and each Volunteer for the restless support.

Having in mind the unanimous YES to the question about wanting to participate in the next AADC (even from the students that will graduate this year!), we can say: see you soon team!!! :)
