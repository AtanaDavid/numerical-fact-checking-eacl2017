Profile & History

Learned game at his father's nine-hole Himley Hall course; taught by the late Alex Lyle, father of Sandy. Met Seve Ballesteros on trip to La Manga when 14. Walker Cup international at 17, Ryder Cup player at 25, winning three points out of four on 1993 debut at The Belfry. Suffered for several seasons after making swing changes. Now back on course.
