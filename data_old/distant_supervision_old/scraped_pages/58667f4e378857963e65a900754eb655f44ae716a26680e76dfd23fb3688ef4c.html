      Surface Design Awards - Recognising excellence

      Recognising examples of progressive design and the use of innovative surfaces in projects both in the UK and internationally the 2017 Finalist list has now been announced.

      Launched in 2013, the Awards have grown quickly both in stature and the number of entrants. 33 projects were named as finalists for the 2017 awards, with 45% being from outside the UK.

      Join us at Surface Design Show to discover who wins and who will take home the title of Supreme Winner.

      The Surface Design Awards 2017

      Meeting in early October to review the entrants, the 2017 judging panel was be Co-Chaired by Ab Rogers Creative Director of Ab Rogers Design and Tina Norden Project Director at Conran and Partners.

      Their judges include: Bethan Wood of Studio Wood, Jenny Jones of Studio Jenny Jones and Sacha Leong from Studioilse.
