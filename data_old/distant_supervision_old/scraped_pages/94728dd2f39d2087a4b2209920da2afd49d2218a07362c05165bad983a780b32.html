3rd Degree at Apr 6, 2014 @ 11:44pm UTC • related • 0 views

Fans of outdoor soccer don't notice a 1-0 scoreline because it happens so often. Indoors, however, a game has ended with a score of 1-0 only 8 times in the 36 years that indoor soccer has been played as a professional sport.

On this date in 1989, the Sidekicks were involved in their first 1-0 game, losing to the Tacoma Stars at Reunion Arena in front of 3,227 fans.
