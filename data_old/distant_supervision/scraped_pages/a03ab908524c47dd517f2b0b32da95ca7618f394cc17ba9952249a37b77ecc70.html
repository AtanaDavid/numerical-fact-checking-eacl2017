Job Summary

We are looking for a web application developer who is motivated to combine the art of design with the art of programming. Responsibilities will include the translation of the UI/UX design specifications to actual code that will produce visual elements of the application. You will work with the Product owner and UI/UX designer taking an active role on both sides and defining how the application looks as well as how it works.

Job Requirements

  • Completed education or equivalent work experience in software development
  • Experience in JavaScript frameworks, especially react.js
  • Expertise creating HTML5 applications
  • Proficient understanding of web markup, including HTML5 and CSS3
  • Excellent English communication skills
  • Positive and self-motivated
  • Open-minded and keen to learn

Pluses

  • Comfortable using git version control
  • Agile development practices
  • Passionate about games, with an understanding of game-play mechanics

Benefits of working at Relax

  • You’re around a lot of really cool & smart people
  • A pretty sweet salary
  • Plenty of opportunities to grow and develop
  • A way to enter gaming industry
  • Super fun team and company events
  • Convenient working schedule
  • Infinite supply of coffee, beverages & snacks

Location

  • Preferred location: Estonia, other locations: negotiable
