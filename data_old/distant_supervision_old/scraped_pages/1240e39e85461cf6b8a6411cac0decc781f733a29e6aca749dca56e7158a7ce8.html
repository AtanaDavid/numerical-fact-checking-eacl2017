Burnley 0-1 Arsenal, Premier League RESULT: Laurent Koscielny nets controversial late winner for Gunners

  • Arsenal earn a stoppage-time win over Burnley at Turf Moor
  • Laurent Koscielny bundled home from close range for the Gunners
  • The defender appeared to handle the ball from an offside position
  • Michael Keane struck the crossbar for Burnley and Sam Vokes went close
  • Gunners register their fifth consecutive Premier League win to go third 
  • Arsene Wenger celebrated his 20th anniversary at the Gunners this week 
  • Burnley XI: Heaton, Lowton, Keane, Mee, Ward, Hendrick, Marney, Defour, Gudmundsson, Boyd, Vokes

Arsenal clinched a last-gasp win away against Burnley after Laurent Koscielny bundled home in injury time at Turf Moor to earn a fifth straight Premier League win.

The Frenchman appeared to handle the ball from an offside position as he deflected the ball in from close range after Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain shot from close range.

It was the perfect way to celebrate Arsene Wenger's 20th year as Arsenal manager as the Gunners rose to third in the league table.  

Relive the action with Sportsmail's JAMES DUTTON, DOMINIC KING and JACK GAUGHAN from Turf Moor...
