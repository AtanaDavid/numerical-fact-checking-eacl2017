Can Canadian Citizens Finance Arizona Real Estate?
 
Here's what Canadians need to know if they want to finance a home purchase in Arizona. Lenders will loan to Canadians to buy an investment home or a vacation home in Arizona. You'll probably need at least 25% down payment for a vacation home and more down if you're buying an investment home. You'll need to have good credit and enough income that the lender is assured that you're a good credit risk. A good realtor will introduce you to several local Arizona lenders. Before you start searching for a home you need to know how much money you can borrow so you know the price of home you can afford. Your lender will fill out a pre-qualification letter that needs to accompany any purchase offer on a home in Arizona.
