Mark Selby

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
Mark Selby at German Masters, 2015

Mark Anthony Selby (born 19 June 1983, Leicester, England) is an English professional snooker and pool player. Selby has won the World Championship, the Masters, the Welsh Open, the Shanghai Masters and is the current world number one.[1]
