Britain’s Got Talent runner-up Jack Carroll tipped for No.1 Christmas DVD

Sarah Deen for Metro.co.ukSunday 9 Jun 2013 11:40 am

Young comedian Jack Carroll, who came second on this year’s Britain’s Got Talent, is being tipped to score a chart-topping Christmas DVD this year.

The youngster is already having a showbiz career mapped out for him by the bookies, as odds of just 3/1 are being offered about him headlining his own arena tour before the end of next year – and he’s 4/1 to top the festive DVD charts.

BGT 2013 winners Attraction are looking to have a promising future too. The artistic Hungarian group are 2/1 to land a Las Vegas residency before the year is out, as reports surface that Simon Cowell hopes to take the group to Sin City.

Alex Donohue of Ladbrokes said of the odds: ‘Attraction and Jack Carroll are now odds-on for super stardom.

‘With Cowell’s clout, Carroll is a firm favourite to become a household name.’

The final of Britain’s Got Talent 2013  saw singing brothers Richard and Adam finish in third place behind Carroll after their performance was interrupted by violinist Natalie Holt throwing eggs at Simon Cowell and the other judges.
