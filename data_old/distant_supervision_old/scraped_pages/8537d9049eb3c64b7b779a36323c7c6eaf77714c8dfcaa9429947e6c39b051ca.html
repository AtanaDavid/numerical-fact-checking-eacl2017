Winner Winner

 

 

Lady Luck has no home here, our aim is to bring the freshest BBQ, burgers and baps around!

All our food is prepared fresh daily by the King of Cooks and co-owner, Michael, who has worked across the UK and travelled through the deep Southern States of America collecting and perfecting only the best recipes and ideas to form unique and delicious WW dishes.

His absolute love of all food, especially the cultural, decadent delights of 'street food', is clear in all his dishes, from the 'Winner Winner Chicken Dinner', (his chicken and waffle special), to a selection of colossal chicken burgers, beasty burritos and fabulous chargrilled steaks. Here in the WW kitchen, we only use the best local produce possible, taking our inspiration and flavours from around the world.
