2017 Boston Marathon Qualifier Acceptances

The B.A.A. continued its notification to applicants of their acceptance into the 2017 Boston Marathon. Qualifiers who were two minutes, nine seconds (2:09) or faster than the qualifying standard for their age group and gender were accepted into next April's race.
