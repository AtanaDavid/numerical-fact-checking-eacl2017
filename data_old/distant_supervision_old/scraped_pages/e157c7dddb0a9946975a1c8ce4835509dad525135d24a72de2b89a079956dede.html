3 October · Willowick, OH, United States ·

🔥🔥🔥 BECOME TEAM BLUE'S GUEST CAPTAIN 🔥🔥🔥

Up for auction is the opportunity to be a GUEST CAPTAIN with the Burning River Roller Derby (BRRD) for Black & Blue 11 ...on Saturday, Nov. 12, 2016! This auction is for Team Blue. Click the link below to place your bid!

As an honorary guest captain you will draft your own roller derby team from BRRD skaters, attend team practices to get to know the skaters and help develop strategy, create lineups, and bench coach your team to victory at Black & Blue 11. One skater representative will be appointed to assist you with your duties before and during the game. Guest captains will also receive 10 free tickets so that friends and family can see you in action, as well as a special thank you gift to remember your experience with the skaters of BRRD.

If you've always wanted to experience roller derby from the inside -- or if you just think your derby knowledge is bound to get you a win -- this is your chance to put your skills to the test and bump elbows with your favorite roller derby skater.

The winning bidder will be contacted within 48 hours of payment to confirm receipt of payment and begin making plans for bout preparation. BRRD will work with the winning bidder to schedule attendance at team practices as our rink schedule allows. If bidders have requests for specific practice dates and times, please message BRRD prior to placing your bid.

The auction for guest captain for rival Team Black is also underway.
