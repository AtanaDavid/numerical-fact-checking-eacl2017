      History

      Established in 2014.

      Robert Foreman is the designated broker for Home & Away Realty, a realty which serves the Phoenix metro area and the White Mountains. Prior to launching Home & Away Realty, Robert worked for HomeSmart Elite, a group of  high-producing agents with a minimum of 5 years' experience, 50 sales over the past 10 years, 10 million dollars over the past 5 years, or 300 sales in their career.

      Meet the Business Owner

      Robert F.
      Business Owner

      Robert Foreman is known as "Robert the Realtor" and "Robert the Lender" to hundreds of homeowners across the Southwest. Helping people purchase their homes has been his passion since 2000.
