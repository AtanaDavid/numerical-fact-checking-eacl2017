    Sevilla in pole position with Dinamo dismantling

    Sevilla took full advantage of a first-half Dinamo Zagreb sending-off to register a 4-0 win, replete with four different scorers, that moved them clear of Juventus in Group H.

    Kampl fires Leverkusen to victory over Tottenham

    Kevin Kampl's opportunistic strike at Wembley secured Bayer Leverkusen a 1-0 victory over Tottenham Hotspur, who drop to third, and second place in Group E.

    Tolisso thwarts Juventus as Lyon earn lifeline

    Corentin Tolisso conjured a late equaliser to prevent Juventus from claiming an early berth in the UEFA Champions League knockout phase and keep Lyon's hopes alive.

    Ramos sees off Sporting to take Dortmund through

    Adrián Ramos scored the only goal early on as Borussia Dortmund edged out Sporting Clube be Portugal to confirm a top-two finish in Group F with two games to spare.
