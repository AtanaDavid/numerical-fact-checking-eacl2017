By Trent Walker 26k votes 2.3k voters 684k views 68 items tags f t p @

List Criteria: Vote up for the celebrity with the most captivating green eyes.

List of the hottest female celebrities with green eyes. These sexy green-eyed stars, which happen to be some of the most beautiful celebrities of all time, are all listed here in this hottest green-eyed women list. Some say it's not easy being green, but the famous babes in the gallery make many women green with envy and make men stare at their beautiful eyes.

Green eye color is caused by a combination of a light brown or amber stroma pigmentation with a low amount of melanin. The hint of blue or aqua tint is the result of the Rayleigh scattering of the reflected light. Green eyes are commonly found in various parts of Europe, the Middle East, and South and Central Asian countries. Green eyes are more prevalent in women than men, so it's no wonder some of Hollywood's hottest stars posses a pair of gorgeous emerald hued eyes.

Sure to be tops in this list of green eyed beauties are actresses like Olivia Wilde, Kristen Stewart, and Rachel McAdams. They are joined by the up-and-coming actresses, pop superstars like Rihanna, and even supermodels such as Tyra Banks and Milla Jovovich. Varying between a grayish green, to a greenish tint with a hazel star-burst, to a dark green, and anywhere in between, these girls truly captivate everyone with just one look.

Who is the hottest female star with stunning green eyes? There are so many gorgeous celebrities to choose from that picking the one celebrity with the best green eyes could be exceedingly difficult, but this alluring green-eyed babes list is here to help you decide. If you don't see your favorite celebrity with green eyes on the list, make sure to add her so others can be taken in by that particular hot celebrity.
