This an archive of Aston
Villa match results and line-ups.
The numbers 1-11 indicates
position ie in a 4-4-2: 
Goalkeeper (1), Rightback
(2), Leftback (3), Central defenders (4 and 5), Right
midfield (7), 
Defensive midfielder (6), Attacking midfielder (8),
Left winger (11), Strikers (9 and 10). 
First substitution is 12 for *, second 14 for ' and
third 15 for''. If a goalkeeper came on 
he will have 13, unless he played as an outfield
player.
