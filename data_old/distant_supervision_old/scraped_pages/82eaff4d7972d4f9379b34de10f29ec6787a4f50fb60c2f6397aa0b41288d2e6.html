UGA Women’s Golf: Lee Is Runner-Up At Spanish Am

06/06/2016 admin

June 5, 2016

SAN SEBASTIAN, Spain — Georgia’s Harang Lee finished as runner-up at the 2016 Spanish Women’s Amateur at the Royal Golf Club of San Sebastian on Sunday. Lee shot 2-under 69 in the final round and completed the 72-hole event at 4-over 288.

Lee posted rounds of 72, 75, 72 and 69. Her Sunday effort was the low score in the final round of the 73 golfers in the field. Lee finished four shots off the pace of Paz Marfa.

Lee posted her fifth top-10s — and seventh top-20s — in the last seven Spanish Ams. She also was runner-up in 2011. In addition, Lee finished sixth in 2010, seventh in 2012, 20th in 2013, 11th in 2014 and ninth in 2015.

A native of Castro Uridales, Spain, recently completed her junior season at Georgia. She earned second-team All-America honors from the Women’s Golf Coaches Association and first-team All-SEC recognition while helping the Bulldogs finish No. 6 in the Golfweek/Sagarin Performance Index.
