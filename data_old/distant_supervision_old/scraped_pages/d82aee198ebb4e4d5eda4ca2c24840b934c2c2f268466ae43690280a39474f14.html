  • Journal Metrics

  • Source Normalized Impact per Paper (SNIP): 0.849 ℹ Source Normalized Impact per Paper (SNIP):
    2015: 0.849
    SNIP measures contextual citation impact by weighting citations based on the total number of citations in a subject field.
  • SCImago Journal Rank (SJR): 0.792 ℹ SCImago Journal Rank (SJR):
    2015: 0.792
    SJR is a prestige metric based on the idea that not all citations are the same. SJR uses a similar algorithm as the Google page rank; it provides a quantitative and a qualitative measure of the journal’s impact.
  • Impact Factor: 1.931 ℹ Impact Factor:
    2015: 1.931
    The Impact Factor measures the average number of citations received in a particular year by papers published in the journal during the two preceding years.
    © Thomson Reuters Journal Citation Reports 2016
  • 5-Year Impact Factor: 1.745 ℹ Five-Year Impact Factor:
    2015: 1.745
    To calculate the five year Impact Factor, citations are counted in 2015 to the previous five years and divided by the source items published in the previous five years.
    © Journal Citation Reports 2016, Published by Thomson Reuters

Winner of the Gerhard Ertl Young Investigator Award 2016 For Excellent Research in Surface Science

At the DPG (The Surface Science Division of the Deutsche Physikalische Gesellschaft) Spring meeting in Darmstadt, the winner for the 2016 Gerhard Ertl Young Investigator Award was chosen. We would like to congratulate Takashi Kumagai from Fritz-Haber-Institut, Berlin, Germany, on this achievement.

Congratulations, Takashi!

We would also like to mention the other finalists - well done, all of you!

Fabio Donati, EPFL, Switzeland

Oliver Hoffmann, University of Graz, Austria

Paolo Sessi, University of Wuerzburg, Germany

Martin Setvin, Technische Hochschule Wien, Austria

(Photo: Publisher Dr. Carina Arasa with the winner and the finalists)
