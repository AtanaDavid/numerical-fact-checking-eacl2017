RSM Classic

17 - 20 November 2016

  • Venue:

    Sea Island, Georgia.

  • Course:

    Seaside Course (Host Course) - 7,000+ yard Par 70 was originally designed by Harry Colt and redesigned by Tom Fazio in 1999. Situated at the southern tip of St. Simons Island it is an American links-style layout. The course has played host to the RSM Classic (formerly known as The McGladrey Classic) since its inauguration in 2010.

  • What Will It Take:

    The fairways are relatively easy to find and the greens are large for a Par 70 layout. Accurate iron-play will be rewarded with Birdie opportunities however the greens are tricky and fast. At just over 7,000 yards, length off the tee is not important and this week is all about GIR and putting. The course can be susceptible to windy conditions.
    RSM Classic Betting Tips & Predictions

  • Defending Champion:

    Kevin Kisner - he beat Kevin Chappell by six strokes shooting a tournament record -22 (260).
    RSM Classic Past Champions

  • How He Did It:

    Kisner finished the week 2nd in Strokes Gained Tee-To-Green, T9 in GIR and 1st in Putts per GIR.
