Urmston Grammar crèche which is run by members of the sixth form is now up and running on Wednesday afternoons. Parents/childminders are welcome to bring their pre-school children. Refreshments and craft activities are provided along with parties at Christmas and Easter. The crèche runs from 1.45 -3.00 pm and the cost is £1.50 per family/group.
