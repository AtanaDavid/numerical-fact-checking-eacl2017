About Us

The Chicago Mustangs, founded in 2012, are a professional indoor soccer team competing in the Major Arena Soccer League (MASL). The Mustangs are the 2014 Ron Newman Cup Champions as well as the 2014 US Open Minifutbol Champions. The Mustangs operate out of the Grand Sports Arena and play their home games at the Sears Centre Arena in Hoffman Estates, IL.

Major Arena Soccer League

The Major Arena Soccer League (MASL) is an international indoor soccer league comprised of teams from the US and Canada. Formed in 2014 after a merger between the PASL and the MISL, the MASL features 22 teams from across the continent and is rapidly expanding. The season is composed of 20 regular season games, 10 home and 10 away.

Practice Facility

The Chicago Mustangs call the Grand Sports Arena their home for practice and team operations. The Grand Sports Arena is a turfed 4-field indoor facility that houses many different recreational leagues and offers out rental space for a variety of teams from a multitude of sports. Grand Sports Arena also features the Red Card Lounge, an upstairs bar with windows to view 2 of the fields as games are played.

Home field

The Chicago Mustangs play their home games at the Sears Centre Arena in Hoffman Estates, IL. The Sears Centre is an 11,800 seat venue that hosts many different events throughout the year. When the indoor soccer field is installed, the Sears Centre has 9,400 seats. The arena also features 43 luxury suites and a private club area with 1,000 seats.
